üìã Your Rewards Optimization Plan

‚úì
Step 1: Check your credit score
Best rewards cards need 700+ score. Check free at Credit Karma. If under 700, build credit first before applying for premium cards.
‚úì
Step 2: Analyze your spending
Where do you spend most? Groceries, gas, dining, travel, Amazon? Look at last 3 months. Pick cards that reward YOUR actual spending.
‚úì
Step 3: Start with one sign-up bonus
Sign-up bonuses are the biggest wins! Make sure you can meet spending requirement with NORMAL spending - don't overspend just for points.
‚úì
Step 4: Use the right card for each purchase
Don't use one card for everything! Use grocery card for groceries, dining card for restaurants, and flat-rate card for everything else.
‚úì
Step 5: Pay in full EVERY month
CRITICAL: Set up autopay for full balance. Interest charges will wipe out all rewards. This only works if you NEVER carry a balance!
‚úì
Step 6: Redeem rewards strategically
Cash back - just cash out. Points - transfer to travel partners for best value (often 1.5-2x). Never redeem for gift cards (bad value).
‚úì
Step 7: Add cards strategically over time
After 6-12 months, add another card for a new bonus. Watch Chase 5/24 rule (no approval if 5+ new cards in 24 months).
‚úì
Step 8: Downgrade instead of closing
When annual fee hits and card isn't worth it, downgrade to no-fee version. Keeps credit history and credit limit active!
