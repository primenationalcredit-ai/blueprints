ğŸ†“ Free & Cheap Growing Tips

ğŸ’¡ Save Money on Seeds & Supplies

  • Regrow from scraps: Green onions, lettuce, celery, herbs can regrow from kitchen scraps
  • Seed libraries: Many libraries have free seed exchanges
  • Dollar store: Seeds, pots, tools for $1 each
  • Free containers: 5-gallon buckets from bakeries (ask nicely!)
  • Compost: Free fertilizer from kitchen scraps
  • Save seeds: Save seeds from this year's harvest for next year

ğŸ’¡ Extend Your Season

  • Start seeds indoors: 6-8 weeks before last frost
  • Cold frames: Extend growing into fall/winter
  • Succession planting: Plant lettuce every 2 weeks for continuous harvest
  • Indoor herbs: Grow year-round on sunny windowsill

âš ï¸ Common Beginner Mistakes

  • Planting too much: Start with 3-5 plants, not 30
  • Wrong location: Veggies need 6-8 hours of direct sun
  • Overwatering: More plants die from too much water than too little
  • Planting too early: Wait until after last frost date
  • Wrong soil: Use potting mix for containers, not garden soil
  • Ignoring spacing: Crowded plants produce less and get diseased
