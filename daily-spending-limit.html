üéØ Why a Daily Limit Works

Monthly budgets are hard to track. But "Can I spend $30 today?" is an easy yes/no question. A daily limit turns budgeting into a simple game you play each day.

üß† The Psychology

When you see your limit every time you open your wallet, it becomes a speed bump before spending. That split-second pause is often enough to stop an impulse purchase.
