üí° Pro Tips

üéØ Make It Work

  • Round down - If math says $43/day, use $40 for buffer
  • Weekly rollover - Didn't spend $10 today? Add to tomorrow or save it
  • Category limits - $15 food, $10 gas, $15 other
  • Zero-spend days - Challenge yourself to 2-3 per week
  • Review weekly - Adjust your limit if needed
