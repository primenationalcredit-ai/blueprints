Step 4: Consider Raising Your Deductible

If you have savings, raising your deductible from $500 to $1,000 can save 15-20% on premiums.

‚ö†Ô∏è Only do this if: You have at least $1,000 in savings to cover a potential claim. Otherwise, keep your lower deductible.

Step 5: Remove Unnecessary Coverage

If your car is older (10+ years, worth less than $5,000), consider dropping collision/comprehensive coverage.

üí° Rule of Thumb: If your annual premium for collision/comprehensive is more than 10% of your car's value, it's probably not worth it.

Step 6: Set Calendar Reminder

Insurance rates change! Set a reminder to shop around every 6-12 months.
