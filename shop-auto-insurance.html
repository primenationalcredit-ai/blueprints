ü§î Why Insurance Shopping Works

Here's what insurance companies don't want you to know:

The truth about auto insurance:

  • Rates vary WILDLY - Same person, same car, prices differ by 50%+ between companies
  • Loyalty isn't rewarded - Long-time customers often pay MORE than new ones
  • "Rate creep" - Companies slowly raise your rate hoping you won't notice
  • Discounts change - You might qualify for new discounts you didn't before
  • Easy to switch - You can switch any time, even mid-policy (you get a refund)

üìã Step-by-Step: Save on Insurance

Follow these steps to potentially save hundreds!

  • Step 1: Get your current policy details
    Find your declarations page (the summary of your coverage). You need this to compare apples to apples.

    Info you'll need:

    • Current coverage levels (liability, collision, comprehensive)
    • Current deductibles
    • Current premium (what you pay)
    • Driver info (ages, driving records)
    • Vehicle info (year, make, model, VIN)
    ‚è±Ô∏è 5 minutes Easy
  • Step 2: Get quotes from comparison sites
    Use sites that give you multiple quotes at once. This saves a TON of time!

    Best comparison sites:

    • The Zebra - Compares 100+ companies, very easy
    • Policygenius - Good for bundling home + auto
    • Gabi - Connects to your current policy automatically
    • NerdWallet - Unbiased comparisons and reviews
    ‚è±Ô∏è 10-15 minutes Easy
  • Step 3: Get direct quotes from 2-3 companies
    Some companies don't work with comparison sites. Get quotes directly from big names.

    Companies to check directly:

    • GEICO - Often cheapest for good drivers
    • Progressive - Good rates, easy online tools
    • State Farm - Great service, competitive rates
    • USAA - Best rates IF you qualify (military connection)
    • Costco/Ameriprise - If you're a Costco member
    ‚è±Ô∏è 15-20 minutes Easy
  • Step 4: Compare quotes (same coverage!)
    Make sure you're comparing the same coverage levels. Write down the best 2-3 quotes.
    ‚è±Ô∏è 10 minutes Important!
  • Step 5: Call your current company with lower quotes
    Before switching, call your current insurer. They might match or beat the price to keep you!

    What to say:

    • "I've been a customer for [X years] and I've been shopping around."
    • "I got a quote from [Company] for [price] with the same coverage."
    • "Is there anything you can do to match that price? I'd prefer to stay with you."
    ‚è±Ô∏è 10-15 minutes Often works!
  • Step 6: Switch or stay - your choice!
    If you find a better deal (or your company won't match), switch! It's easy and you'll get a refund for unused premium.
    ‚è±Ô∏è 15 minutes Congrats on your savings!
