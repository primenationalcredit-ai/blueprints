üìã Your Voice Acting Action Plan

‚úì
Step 1: Set up your recording space
Find a quiet space - closets work great! Get USB condenser mic ($100-200), pop filter, headphones. DIY acoustic treatment with blankets if needed.
‚úì
Step 2: Practice and train your voice
Read aloud daily! Practice different styles: commercial, narration, character. Record yourself, listen back, improve. Consider voice coaching.
‚úì
Step 3: Learn audio editing basics
Use Audacity (free). Learn noise reduction, normalization, removing breaths/clicks. Clients expect clean, polished audio!
‚úì
Step 4: Create your demo reel
60-90 seconds showcasing your range. Commercial, narration, character styles. This is your calling card - make it professional!
‚úì
Step 5: Create profiles on platforms
Voices.com, Voice123, Fiverr, ACX (audiobooks). Upload demo, complete profile, set competitive rates to start!
‚úì
Step 6: Audition relentlessly
Submit 10-20+ auditions per week. It's a numbers game! Customize each audition - don't send generic reads.
‚úì
Step 7: Deliver professional quality
Meet deadlines, provide clean audio, communicate clearly. Quick revisions. Happy clients = repeat work and referrals!
‚úì
Step 8: Build direct client relationships
After success on platforms, pursue direct clients. Marketing agencies, e-learning companies, production studios. Higher rates, no platform fees!
