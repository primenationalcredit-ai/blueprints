üìã Your REIT Investing Action Plan

‚úì
Step 1: Learn REIT basics
Understand how REITs work, different property types, and why they pay high dividends (90% payout requirement). Watch YouTube videos on REIT investing.
‚úì
Step 2: Decide: traded REITs vs. private
Traded REITs (on stock exchanges) are more liquid. Private REITs (Fundrise, etc.) may have higher yields but less liquidity. Beginners: start with traded REITs or REIT ETFs!
‚úì
Step 3: Open appropriate account
For traded REITs: Fidelity, Schwab, Vanguard. Consider a Roth IRA for tax benefits! For private REITs: sign up for Fundrise or similar.
‚úì
Step 4: Start with a REIT ETF (easiest)
VNQ (Vanguard Real Estate ETF) gives you instant diversification across dozens of REITs. One purchase, instant real estate exposure!
‚úì
Step 5: Research individual REITs (optional)
Once comfortable, research individual REITs. Look at: dividend history, payout ratio, property quality, management, and FFO (funds from operations).
‚úì
Step 6: Enable dividend reinvestment
Set up DRIP to automatically reinvest dividends. Your REIT dividends buy more shares, which pay more dividends. Compounding!
‚úì
Step 7: Consider tax-advantaged accounts
REIT dividends are taxed as ordinary income. Hold REITs in a Roth IRA if possible - tax-free growth and withdrawals!
‚úì
Step 8: Add consistently and diversify
Invest regularly. Diversify across property types (residential, industrial, healthcare, etc.). Long-term thinking wins!
