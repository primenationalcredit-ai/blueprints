Egg donation = higher pay but more intensive. Sperm donation = lower per visit but ongoing income. Consider your situation, time, and commitment level.

Age, health, BMI, lifestyle (non-smoker, etc.). Requirements vary by agency/bank. Don't be discouraged - apply to multiple places!

You'll need to visit in person. Look for reputable, established organizations. Compare compensation rates and requirements.

Step 4: Submit applications
Apply to multiple agencies/banks. Applications ask about health history, education, lifestyle. Include good photos (egg donation especially).
‚úì
Step 5: Complete screening process
Medical exams, genetic testing, psychological screening (egg donation), infectious disease testing. All costs covered by agency/bank!
‚úì
Step 6: Get matched/accepted
Egg donors: wait for intended parents to choose you. Sperm donors: if samples pass quality testing, you're accepted into program.
‚úì
Step 7: Complete donation cycle
Egg: 2-3 week medication cycle + retrieval procedure. Sperm: Regular donations 1-3x per week. Follow all instructions carefully!
‚úì
Step 8: Receive compensation
Egg: Payment after retrieval ($5,000-15,000+). Sperm: Paid per donation ($70-150). Consider future cycles if eligible!
