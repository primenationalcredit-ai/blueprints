üìã Your Tax Prep Action Plan

‚úì
Step 1: Get your IRS PTIN
Apply at IRS.gov for your Preparer Tax Identification Number. It's FREE and required for anyone who prepares returns for compensation. Renew annually before tax season!
‚úì
Step 2: Complete tax preparation training
Take a comprehensive course: H&R Block Income Tax Course, Jackson Hewitt training, or online programs like Surgent or Gleim. Learn tax law, forms, and software. Usually 60-80 hours.
‚úì
Step 3: Choose your tax software
Professional software options: Drake Tax, Intuit ProConnect, TaxSlayer Pro, TaxAct Professional. Compare pricing (per-return vs. unlimited). Many offer free trials!
‚úì
Step 4: Get your EFIN (if e-filing directly)
Apply for Electronic Filing Identification Number at IRS.gov if you want to e-file returns directly. Takes 45+ days to process - apply early! Or work under someone else's EFIN initially.
‚úì
Step 5: Set up your practice
Get E&O insurance ($200-500/year). Set your pricing. Create intake forms. Decide: home office, client visits, or virtual? Secure document storage is essential!
‚úì
Step 6: Market your services
Start marketing in December/January! Nextdoor, Facebook, Google Business. Tell everyone you know. Offer early-bird discounts. Flyers at local businesses!
‚úì
Step 7: Prepare returns and build clientele
Start with simple returns, work up to complex. Double-check everything! Maximize refunds legally. Happy clients refer friends and return next year!
‚úì
Step 8: Expand services year-round
Add bookkeeping, payroll, quarterly estimates, tax planning. Become an Enrolled Agent for advanced credentials. Build year-round income beyond tax season!
