üìã Step-by-Step: Cut Your Costs

  • Step 1: List ALL your TV/streaming subscriptions
    Check your bank statement. Include cable, streaming apps, live TV services - everything!
    ‚è±Ô∏è 10 minutes Eye-opening!
  • Step 2: If you have cable - CUT IT
    Cable at $100-200/month is almost never worth it anymore. Everything is available cheaper through streaming.

    For live TV/sports:

    • YouTube TV - $73/month (all major channels + sports)
    • Hulu + Live TV - $77/month
    • Sling TV - $40/month (cheaper, fewer channels)
    • Free antenna - $0/month for local channels!
    ‚è±Ô∏è 15 minutes to call Biggest savings!
  • Step 3: Use the "rotation" strategy
    Don't pay for 5 services at once! Subscribe to ONE, binge what you want, cancel, move to the next.

    How rotation works:

    • Month 1: Netflix - watch your shows, cancel
    • Month 2: HBO Max - watch their originals, cancel
    • Month 3: Disney+ - catch up on Marvel/Star Wars, cancel
    • Cost: $15-20/month instead of $60+!
    ‚è±Ô∏è Ongoing Saves 60-70%!
  • Step 4: Use FREE streaming services
    There are TONS of free (legal!) streaming options with great content. See list below!
    ‚è±Ô∏è 5 minutes to set up $0/month!
  • Step 5: Share accounts (where allowed)
    Many services allow multiple profiles. Split costs with family members!
    ‚è±Ô∏è Varies Saves 50%
  • Step 6: Downgrade to ad-supported tiers
    Netflix, Hulu, Disney+, Max all have cheaper ad-supported options. Same content, lower price!

    Ad-supported savings:

    • Netflix: $7 vs $15 (save $8/month)
    • Hulu: $8 vs $18 (save $10/month)
    • Disney+: $8 vs $14 (save $6/month)
    • Max: $10 vs $16 (save $6/month)
