‚ö° Speed Shopping Tips

How Top Shoppers Hit 30-40 Seconds/Item

1

Group by aisle: Mentally sort your list before starting. Don't zigzag across the store.

2

Learn item locations: After a few shifts, you'll know exactly where everything is. This is your biggest speed boost.

3

Scan while walking: Don't stop, scan, then move. Scan AS you grab and keep moving.

4

Use two hands: Grab with one, scan with other. Every second counts.

5

Know replacement rules: Have common substitutions memorized. Don't waste time messaging for obvious swaps.

6

Strategic cart loading: Heavy items bottom, fragile top. Organized cart = faster checkout.

üí° Pro Shopper Tips

  • Peak shift times: Morning (9-11 AM) and evening (5-7 PM) usually have most orders
  • Build rapport: Friendly with store staff = they help you find items faster
  • Check markdowns: Know where clearance sections are for common replacement options
  • Stay hydrated: Bring a water bottle. You're walking constantly‚Äîdon't drain yourself
  • Dress comfortably: Stores are cold. Layers work. Comfortable = faster.

‚ö†Ô∏è Things to Watch Out For

  • Hour caps: Instacart In-Store caps at 29 hours/week (to avoid benefits)
  • Metrics matter: Speed AND accuracy ratings affect your shift access
  • Heavy items: Be prepared to lift cases of water, pet food, etc.
  • Standing time: Some stores have waiting periods between orders
  • No tips (usually): In-store shoppers typically don't receive customer tips
