üìã Your Tutoring Action Plan

‚úì
Step 1: Identify your best subjects
What did you ace? High test scores? Languages? Skills like coding or music? You can tutor levels below your expertise - college students can tutor high school!
‚úì
Step 2: Set up your tutoring space
Quiet room, good lighting, reliable internet, webcam. Professional background (bookshelf, clean wall). Good microphone helps!
‚úì
Step 3: Sign up on tutoring platforms
Start with Wyzant and Tutor.com. Complete profiles fully! Add all subjects you can teach. Include any test scores, degrees, experience.
‚úì
Step 4: Price competitively to start
Start lower to build reviews ($20-30/hr for most subjects). After 5-10 great reviews, raise rates! Reviews are everything on platforms.
‚úì
Step 5: Deliver amazing sessions
Be patient, prepared, encouraging. Celebrate student wins! Great sessions = 5-star reviews = more students = higher rates.
‚úì
Step 6: Ask for reviews & referrals
After successful sessions, ask for platform reviews. Ask parents for referrals. Word of mouth grows your business!
‚úì
Step 7: Build private client base
As you get referrals, take clients privately (keep 100% vs platform's 25% cut). But honor platform rules - don't poach their clients!
‚úì
Step 8: Raise rates & specialize
After 20+ great reviews, raise rates gradually. Consider specializing (test prep pays best). Build recurring weekly students!
