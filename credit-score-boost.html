üìã Your Credit Boost Action Plan

‚úì
Step 1: Check your credit reports for errors
Get free reports from AnnualCreditReport.com. 34% of people have errors! Look for wrong accounts, incorrect balances, or accounts that aren't yours. Dispute errors with each bureau.
‚úì
Step 2: Lower your credit utilization
Get under 30% (ideally under 10%). This can boost your score 20-50+ points in ONE billing cycle! Pay down balances or request limit increases.
‚úì
Step 3: Set up autopay for EVERYTHING
Never miss a payment again! Set up autopay for at least the minimum on all accounts. Payment history is 35% of your score.
‚úì
Step 4: Sign up for Experian Boost
FREE! Get credit for bills you already pay: utilities, phone, streaming services. Can add 10-20+ points instantly!
‚úì
Step 5: Become an authorized user
Ask a family member with excellent credit to add you to their oldest card. You inherit their payment history! Just make sure they have good history.
‚úì
Step 6: Request credit limit increases
Higher limits = lower utilization (if you don't spend more). Call your card issuers and ask! Many do this without a hard pull.
‚úì
Step 7: Don't close old cards
Closing cards hurts your score by increasing utilization and shortening credit history. Downgrade to no-fee version instead of closing.
‚úì
Step 8: Be patient and consistent
Some changes take time. Keep paying on time, keep utilization low, and your score will climb. Check monthly to track progress!

üö´ Credit Score Myths - Busted!

MYTH: "Checking my credit hurts my score"
TRUTH: Checking your own credit is a "soft inquiry" and has NO impact. Check often!
MYTH: "I need to carry a balance to build credit"
TRUTH: You do NOT need to pay interest! Pay in full every month.
MYTH: "Closing old cards helps my score"
TRUTH: Closing cards HURTS your score by increasing utilization and shortening history.
MYTH: "I only have one credit score"
TRUTH: You have dozens! FICO, VantageScore, and each bureau calculates differently.
