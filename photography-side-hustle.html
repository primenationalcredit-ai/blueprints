üõ†Ô∏è Equipment to Start

Budget Start (Under $500):

  • Smartphone - Modern phones take great photos! Start here.
  • Entry DSLR/Mirrorless - Canon Rebel, Nikon D3500, Sony a6000 ($300-500 used)
  • 50mm f/1.8 lens - "Nifty fifty" is cheap and great for portraits ($100-200)

Upgrade Later:

  • Better camera body
  • Additional lenses (35mm, 85mm)
  • External flash/lighting
  • Editing software (Lightroom)
