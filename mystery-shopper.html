‚úì
Step 1: Sign up with all legitimate companies above
Register with EVERY company listed above. It's free to join all of them. More companies = more shop opportunities in your area. Some shops are exclusive to certain companies!
‚úì
Step 2: Complete your shopper profile 100%
Fill out ALL demographics, interests, availability, and location info. Complete profiles get more shop offers! Companies match shops to your profile details.
‚úì
Step 3: Take any required certifications
Some companies require a certification quiz before you can shop. These are usually easy multiple choice about guidelines. Complete them to unlock more shops!
‚úì
Step 4: Start with easy beginner shops
Gas stations, fast food, and quick retail visits are best for beginners. They're short, requirements are simple, and you build your reputation with quality reports.
‚úì
Step 5: Read instructions VERY carefully before each shop
Every shop has specific requirements - what to order, questions to ask, what to observe. Missing a requirement can get your shop rejected. Read twice!
‚úì
Step 6: Submit detailed, accurate reports on time
Quality reports = higher shopper rating = more shop assignments. Be objective, detailed, and ALWAYS submit before the deadline. Late = rejected = no pay!
‚úì
Step 7: Check for new shops daily and grab good ones fast
Good shops (especially restaurant and hotel) get claimed within hours! Check your dashboards daily. Set up email/text alerts. First come, first served!
‚úì
Step 8: Route shops efficiently for maximum earnings
Combine multiple shops in the same area on the same trip. A gas station + fast food + retail in one outing maximizes your hourly rate. Plan your routes!

üí° Pro Tips

üí° Bonused Shops = Extra Money

When shops go unclaimed near their deadline, companies add bonuses - sometimes 2-3x the normal pay! Check for "bonused" or "urgent" shops for the best rates.

üí° Take Notes Immediately

Write down employee names, times, and details RIGHT AFTER your visit while fresh in your mind. Some shoppers record voice memos in their car. Don't rely on memory!
