üìã Your Mystery Shopping Action Plan

‚úì
Step 1: Sign up with multiple legitimate companies
Register with BestMark, Market Force, IntelliShop, Secret Shopper, and Sinclair. More companies = more shop opportunities in your area!
‚úì
Step 2: Complete your profile thoroughly
Fill out demographics, vehicle info, and availability. More complete profiles get matched with more shops. List any special skills (car knowledge, banking experience, etc.)
‚úì
Step 3: Start with easy shops
Gas stations, fast food, and simple retail shops are great for beginners. Build your reputation with quality reports before tackling complex assignments.
‚úì
Step 4: Read requirements CAREFULLY
Each shop has specific instructions - what to order, questions to ask, what to observe. Missing a requirement can disqualify your shop and you won't get paid!
‚úì
Step 5: Take notes and save receipts
Note employee names, timestamps, and specific observations. Take photos of receipts immediately. You'll need these details for your report!
‚úì
Step 6: Submit detailed, accurate reports
Quality reports = more shop assignments. Be objective, specific, and submit on time (usually within 24 hours). Proofread before submitting!
‚úì
Step 7: Check for shops regularly
Good shops get claimed fast! Check daily. Set up email alerts. Best shops often post on weekends or end of month when quotas need filling.
‚úì
Step 8: Route shops efficiently
Combine multiple shops in the same area on one trip. 3-4 shops in one afternoon = maximized earnings and minimized driving time!
