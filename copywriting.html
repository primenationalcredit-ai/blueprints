üìã Your Copywriting Action Plan

‚úì
Step 1: Learn copywriting fundamentals
Study "The Boron Letters," "Breakthrough Advertising." Learn frameworks: AIDA (Attention, Interest, Desire, Action), PAS (Problem, Agitation, Solution). Free resources: Copyblogger, Copy Hackers.
‚úì
Step 2: Choose your niche
Specialists earn more! Pick an industry (SaaS, health, finance) OR a format (emails, sales pages, ads). "Email copywriter for SaaS" beats "freelance writer."
‚úì
Step 3: Build your portfolio
No clients yet? Write spec pieces! Rewrite existing ads/pages. Create samples for fictional companies. 3-5 strong samples is all you need to start.
‚úì
Step 4: Create your online presence
LinkedIn profile optimized for copywriting. Simple portfolio website (Carrd, Notion). Write about copywriting on LinkedIn - content marketing works!
‚úì
Step 5: Find your first clients
Cold email businesses with weak copy. Apply on Upwork, Contently. Join communities (Copy Hackers, Peak Freelance). Network on LinkedIn!
‚úì
Step 6: Deliver results-focused copy
Focus on client's goals, not just words. Ask about their metrics. When possible, track results - "Email sequence increased conversions 35%." Results = testimonials!
‚úì
Step 7: Get testimonials & referrals
After every project, ask for testimonial. Ask happy clients for referrals. One great client can lead to 3-4 more through referrals!
‚úì
Step 8: Build recurring revenue
Convert one-off clients to retainers. Monthly email work, ongoing ad campaigns, content calendars. 3 retainer clients = stable business!
