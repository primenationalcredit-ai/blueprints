üìã Your Transcription Action Plan

‚úì
Step 1: Test your typing speed
Go to typingtest.com - you need 60+ WPM minimum, 70+ is better. Practice daily if needed! Speed directly impacts your hourly rate.
‚úì
Step 2: Get the right equipment
Quality headphones are essential! A foot pedal ($30-50) speeds things up significantly. Install Express Scribe (free) or oTranscribe.
‚úì
Step 3: Learn transcription standards
Learn formatting: timestamps, speaker labels, [inaudible] tags, verbatim vs clean transcription. Free resources online!
‚úì
Step 4: Practice on free content
Transcribe YouTube videos or podcasts for practice. Focus on accuracy first, then speed. Build your muscle memory!
‚úì
Step 5: Apply to transcription platforms
Start with Rev, TranscribeMe, GoTranscript. They have entrance tests but are beginner-friendly. Apply to multiple!
‚úì
Step 6: Build speed and accuracy
As you work, you'll get faster. Track your audio-minute-to-real-time ratio. Use keyboard shortcuts religiously!
‚úì
Step 7: Level up to better platforms
After building skills, apply to higher-paying platforms. Direct clients through Upwork/Fiverr pay better than entry platforms.
‚úì
Step 8: Consider specialization
Medical and legal transcription pay 50-100% more. Training required but worth it for serious income. Look into certification programs!
