üéØ What You'll Achieve

The Debt Snowball is a debt payoff strategy where you pay off debts from smallest to largest, regardless of interest rate. By knocking out small debts first, you build psychological momentum and motivation that keeps you going until you're completely debt-free. Research from Harvard Business Review proves this method works because of the "small wins" psychology!

"Personal finance is 80% behavior, 20% math"

Quick wins keep you motivated. Motivation keeps you going. That's why the snowball works.

üìö How the Debt Snowball Works

1

List ALL Your Debts (Smallest to Largest)

Write down every debt you have except your mortgage. Order them by balance, smallest first - ignore interest rates for now!

2

Make Minimum Payments on Everything

Pay the minimum on all debts to stay current and avoid late fees and credit damage.

3

Attack the Smallest Debt with EVERYTHING Extra

Every extra dollar goes to your smallest debt. Cut expenses, pick up a side hustle - throw it all at debt #1!

4

Roll the Payment to the Next Debt

When the smallest debt is paid off, take that entire payment and add it to the minimum of your next smallest debt.

5

Repeat Until Debt-Free! üéâ

Your payment amount grows like a snowball rolling downhill. Each debt falls faster than the last!

‚öñÔ∏è Snowball vs. Avalanche: Which Is Better?

‚ùÑÔ∏è Debt Snowball (Recommended)

  • Pay smallest balance first
  • Quick wins = high motivation
  • Proven by Harvard research
  • Higher success rate (people stick with it)
  • May pay slightly more interest
  • Best for: Most people!

üèîÔ∏è Debt Avalanche

  • Pay highest interest first
  • Saves more money mathematically
  • Takes longer to see progress
  • Many people quit before finishing
  • Requires strong self-discipline
  • Best for: Math-focused people
üí° The Bottom Line

The best debt payoff method is the one you'll actually stick with! If you're motivated by quick wins, use the Snowball. If math drives you and you're disciplined, try the Avalanche. Either way, the key is to START and KEEP GOING.
