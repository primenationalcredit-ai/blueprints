üìã Your eBook Publishing Action Plan

‚úì
Step 1: Choose a profitable niche
Research what sells! Check Amazon bestseller lists in your category. Non-fiction how-to often outperforms fiction. Find topics with demand but not oversaturated.
‚úì
Step 2: Outline your book
Create detailed outline first. Non-fiction: 10-15 chapters. Break each chapter into sections. This makes writing much easier!
‚úì
Step 3: Write your book
Non-fiction can be 10,000-30,000 words. Fiction typically 40,000+. Write 1,000-2,000 words daily. Consistency is key! Or hire ghostwriter ($500-2,000).
‚úì
Step 4: Edit and proofread
Typos KILL reviews! Use Grammarly, hire an editor on Fiverr ($50-200), or get beta readers. Edit multiple times. Fresh eyes help!
‚úì
Step 5: Create a professional cover
Covers sell books! Hire designer on Fiverr ($20-100) or use Canva. Study top-selling covers in your niche. Must look good at thumbnail size!
‚úì
Step 6: Format and publish on KDP
Create KDP account (free). Use Kindle Create for formatting. Write keyword-rich description. Choose 7 keywords, select categories. Price at $2.99-$4.99 for 70% royalty!
‚úì
Step 7: Get reviews and market
Ask readers for reviews (in back matter). Run Amazon AMS ads ($5-10/day). Share on social media. Reviews drive sales!
‚úì
Step 8: Publish more books!
More books = more income. Create series when possible. Build an email list for launches. Think catalog, not single book! Each book promotes the others.
