The 60-70% Pricing Rule

Price to SELL, Not to Get Rich

Price at 60-70% of what similar items are selling for. You want FAST cash, not maximum profit. Check "Sold" items on Facebook Marketplace to see real prices.

Step 1: Find Items to Sell

  • Electronics: Old phones, tablets, laptops, gaming systems
  • Furniture: Anything in good condition you don't use
  • Baby/Kid items: Strollers, cribs, toys, clothes
  • Exercise equipment: Weights, bikes, treadmills
  • Tools: Power tools sell same day
  • Designer items: Bags, shoes, clothes
  • Appliances: Working small appliances
  • Sports equipment: Golf clubs, bikes, etc.

Step 2: Take Great Photos (Sells 3X Faster)

  • Clean the item thoroughly first
  • Use natural daylight (near a window)
  • Take 4-6 photos from different angles
  • Show any flaws honestly
  • Include size reference (put a shoe or hand next to it)
  • Clear background (no clutter)

Step 3: Write Listings That Sell

  • Start title with brand name if applicable
  • Include key specs (size, color, condition)
  • Mention "OBO" (or best offer) to attract more buyers
  • Add "Cross-posted" so buyers act fast
  • State pickup location (neighborhood, not exact address)

Step 4: Post & Respond Fast

  • List on BOTH Facebook Marketplace AND OfferUp
  • Respond to messages within 15 minutes
  • First person with cash in hand gets it
  • Don't hold items unless they Venmo a deposit
