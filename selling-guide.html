üìù How to Sell Successfully

‚úì

Step 1: Gather Your Items

Go room by room. If you haven't used it in 6 months, it goes in the sell pile.

‚úì

Step 2: Research Prices

Search "sold" listings on eBay/Mercari. See what similar items ACTUALLY sold for.

‚úì

Step 3: Take Great Photos

Natural lighting. Clean background. Multiple angles. Photos sell items!

‚úì

Step 4: Write Clear Descriptions

Brand, size, condition, flaws. Include measurements. Be honest.

‚úì

Step 5: Price Competitively

Price 10-20% below similar listings to sell fast. Or price high and accept offers.

‚úì

Step 6: Respond Quickly

Fast responses = more sales. Answer within an hour if possible.

‚úì

Step 7: Complete the Sale

Ship promptly or arrange safe pickup. Leave reviews. Build reputation.

üí° Pro Selling Tips

  • List on multiple platforms: More eyes = faster sales
  • Bundle items: "Take all 3 for $50" moves slow sellers
  • Relist weekly: Refresh listings to boost visibility
  • Accept reasonable offers: 80% of asking is still money in pocket
  • Meet in safe locations: Police station parking lots, busy public areas

‚ö†Ô∏è Avoid These Mistakes

  • Bad photos: Dark, blurry photos kill sales
  • Overpricing: Check what items ACTUALLY sell for, not asking prices
  • Slow responses: Buyers move on quickly
  • Not disclosing flaws: Leads to returns and bad reviews
  • Unsafe meetups: Never invite strangers to your home alone
