üè¶ Financing Options

FHA Loan (3.5% down)

Best for beginners! Low down payment, allows 1-4 unit properties if you live in one. Must live there 1 year.

üí∞ Lowest down payment

Conventional (5-20% down)

No PMI at 20% down. Also allows house hacking on 1-4 units. More flexibility after closing.

üè† Standard option

VA Loan (0% down)

For veterans/military. Zero down payment! Allows up to 4 units if you live in one. Best deal if you qualify!

üí° Why Fourplex is the Sweet Spot

4 units is the maximum you can buy with residential (FHA/VA/Conventional) financing. 5+ units requires commercial loans with higher down payments. A fourplex with 3 tenants can often fully cover your mortgage AND give you cash flow!
