‚ÑπÔ∏è What Is Credit Card Stacking?

Credit card stacking is the strategy of applying for multiple business credit cards in a short window (same day or same week) before the inquiries show on your credit report. This allows you to get approved for multiple cards at higher limits before any single issuer sees the other applications.

  • Why it works: Hard inquiries take 24-48 hours to appear on credit reports
  • Typical results: $50K-$150K+ in combined credit limits
  • Best timing: Apply to all cards on the SAME DAY for maximum success
