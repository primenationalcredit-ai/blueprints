üìÖ Week 1: Foundation (Days 1-7)

üîç Know Your Starting Point Foundation Week

Before you can go anywhere, you need to know where you are. This week is about understanding your current financial picture.

  • Day 1: Calculate your net worth (assets - debts)
  • Day 2: List all your debts with interest rates
  • Day 3: Check if you have a 401(k) available at work
  • Day 4: Find out if your employer offers a match (FREE MONEY!)
  • Day 5: Calculate your monthly "investable" amount (after bills)
  • Day 6: Decide: 401(k), IRA, or taxable brokerage?
  • Day 7: Rest and review your notes

üèÜ Week 1 Milestone

You know exactly where you stand and which account to open

üìÖ Week 2: Research (Days 8-14)

üìö Learn the Basics Research Week

Knowledge is power. This week you'll learn just enough to act confidently - not so much that you get overwhelmed.

  • Day 8: Learn what an index fund is (15 min YouTube video)
  • Day 9: Understand the difference between Roth and Traditional
  • Day 10: Compare 3 brokerages (Fidelity, Schwab, Vanguard)
  • Day 11: Look up the expense ratio on VTI, VOO, or FXAIX
  • Day 12: Read one article on "dollar cost averaging"
  • Day 13: Calculate how much $100/mo grows in 30 years
  • Day 14: Pick your brokerage and first fund

üèÜ Week 2 Milestone

You've chosen your brokerage and know which fund to buy

üìÖ Week 3: Action (Days 15-21)

‚ö° Open Your Account Action Week

This is it! The week you go from thinking about investing to actually doing it. One step at a time.

  • Day 15: Start the account opening process (have ID ready)
  • Day 16: Link your bank account for transfers
  • Day 17: Wait for account approval (use time to review)
  • Day 18: Set up your first transfer ($25-100)
  • Day 19: Make your first investment purchase!
  • Day 20: Set up automatic recurring investments
  • Day 21: Celebrate! You're officially an investor!

üèÜ Week 3 Milestone

You own shares of an index fund! üéâ

üìÖ Week 4: Habit (Days 22-30)

üîÑ Build the Habit Habit Week

One investment doesn't build wealth - consistent investing does. This week is about making it automatic.

  • Day 22: Verify automatic investment is scheduled
  • Day 23: Set calendar reminder to check in monthly (not daily!)
  • Day 24: Delete any stock trading apps (avoid temptation)
  • Day 25: Tell someone about your new investing habit
  • Day 26: Calculate your 1-year and 5-year projection
  • Day 27: Write your "future self" a letter about why you started
  • Day 28: Research one way to increase your investment amount
  • Day 29: Review your 30-day journey
  • Day 30: Plan your next financial goal

üèÜ Week 4 Milestone

Automatic investing is set up - wealth building is now on autopilot!

üí° Pro Tips for Success

  • Start smaller than you think: $25/week beats $0/week every time
  • Automate everything: Willpower runs out, automation doesn't
  • Don't check daily: The market goes up AND down - that's normal
  • Time beats timing: Starting now beats waiting for the "perfect" time
  • Boring is good: Index funds aren't exciting - and that's the point

‚ö†Ô∏è Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Waiting to start: "I'll invest when I make more money" - start NOW
  • Picking individual stocks: Index funds outperform most stock pickers
  • Checking too often: Daily checking leads to emotional decisions
  • Stopping during dips: Market drops are sales - keep investing!
  • Skipping employer match: That's literally free money you're refusing
