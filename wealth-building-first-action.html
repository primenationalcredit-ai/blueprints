Retirement Account First Action
‚è±Ô∏è 30-60 minutes

Your 30-Day Goal: Start Contributing to a 401(k) or Open a Roth IRA

Week 1: Research & Decide
  • Check if your employer offers a 401(k)
  • Find out if there's an employer match (FREE MONEY!)
  • Get enrollment paperwork from HR
  • If no 401(k): Decide between Roth IRA or Traditional IRA
Week 2: Take Action

If you have a 401(k):

  • Complete enrollment paperwork
  • Set contribution to AT LEAST get full employer match
  • Choose a Target Date Fund for your retirement year

If opening a Roth IRA:

  • Go to Fidelity.com, Vanguard.com, or Schwab.com
  • Click "Open an Account" ‚Üí Select "Roth IRA"
  • Complete application (need SSN, bank info)
  • Link your bank account
Week 3: Fund It
  • Make your FIRST contribution (even $50 counts!)
  • Set up automatic monthly contributions
  • Invest in a Total Market Index Fund or Target Date Fund
Week 4: Optimize
  • Confirm contributions are being deducted
  • Verify money is invested (not sitting in cash!)
  • Plan to increase contribution by 1% every raise
üéâ Success = You've made your first retirement contribution and set up automatic investing. Even $50/month = $150,000+ over 30 years!

Index Fund First Action
‚è±Ô∏è 20-30 minutes

Your 30-Day Goal: Open a Brokerage Account and Buy Your First Index Fund

Week 1: Open Account
  • Choose a broker: Fidelity, Vanguard, or Schwab (all great, free)
  • Go to their website and click "Open an Account"
  • Select "Individual Brokerage Account"
  • Complete application (10-15 minutes)
  • Link your bank account for transfers
Week 2: Fund & Learn
  • Transfer money to your brokerage (start with $100+)
  • Learn what these funds are:
    • VTI / FSKAX / SWTSX = Total US Stock Market
    • VOO / FXAIX / SWPPX = S&P 500 (top 500 companies)
Week 3: Buy Your First Shares!
  • Search for a Total Market Fund (VTI, FSKAX, or SWTSX)
  • Click "Buy" or "Trade"
  • Enter dollar amount (can buy fractional shares!)
  • Confirm and submit order
  • üéâ YOU'RE NOW AN INVESTOR!
Week 4: Automate
  • Set up automatic monthly investment
  • Choose amount you can afford consistently
  • Pick same day each month (after payday!)
üéâ Success = You own shares of a total market index fund and have automatic investing set up!

Real Estate First Action
‚è±Ô∏è Varies by approach

Choose Your Starting Point:

Option A: Start with REITs (Easiest - $100 to start)

Real Estate Investment Trusts let you invest in real estate like buying stocks.

  • Open brokerage account (Fidelity, Vanguard, Schwab)
  • Search for a REIT fund: VNQ (Vanguard Real Estate ETF) or FREL (Fidelity)
  • Buy your first shares ($100+)
  • Set up monthly automatic investment

Option B: Learn House Hacking (Medium - Future purchase)

Buy a 2-4 unit property, live in one unit, rent the others.

  • Check your credit score (need 620+ for FHA loan)
  • Research FHA loans (3.5% down payment!)
  • Look at 2-4 unit properties in your area on Zillow/Redfin
  • Calculate potential rent income vs mortgage
  • Talk to a lender about pre-approval

Option C: Rental Property Analysis (Advanced)

Learn to analyze rental property deals.

  • Learn the 1% rule (monthly rent should be ~1% of purchase price)
  • Analyze 5 properties on Zillow using the 1% rule
  • Research rental rates in your target area
  • Calculate cash-on-cash return for one property
  • Join a local real estate investor meetup
üéâ Success = You've either invested in REITs OR completed analysis on real properties in your market!

Business First Action
‚è±Ô∏è 2-4 hours

Your 30-Day Goal: Make Your First $1 from a Side Business

üí° The $1 Challenge

Your first dollar from YOUR business is the hardest. Once you've made $1, you can make $100. Once $100, you can make $1,000. Just get to $1!

Week 1: Identify Your Skill

What can you do that others would pay for?

  • List 5 skills you have (cleaning, writing, design, repairs, tutoring, etc.)
  • Ask 3 friends: "What would you pay me to help with?"
  • Choose ONE service to offer
  • Set a simple price (start low to get first customers)
Week 2: Tell People
  • Post on Facebook: "I'm offering [service] for [price]. DM me!"
  • Post on Nextdoor
  • Tell 10 friends/family members personally
  • Create a simple flyer (Canva.com - free!)
  • Post flyer in local Facebook groups
Week 3: Get Your First Customer
  • Offer a discount to first customer ("50% off first job!")
  • Ask friends if they know anyone who needs your service
  • Follow up with anyone who showed interest
  • BOOK YOUR FIRST CUSTOMER!
Week 4: Deliver & Learn
  • Complete the job for your first customer
  • Collect payment (cash, Venmo, Zelle)
  • Ask for a review/testimonial
  • Ask: "Do you know anyone else who needs this?"
  • üéâ YOU'VE MADE YOUR FIRST $1!
üéâ Success = You've made money from YOUR OWN business! Now scale it!

Dividend Investing First Action
‚è±Ô∏è 30-45 minutes

Your 30-Day Goal: Buy Your First Dividend-Paying Investment

Week 1: Open Account & Learn
  • Open brokerage account (Fidelity, Vanguard, or Schwab)
  • Learn what "dividend yield" means (annual dividend √∑ stock price)
  • Understand: Dividends are paid quarterly (4x per year)
Week 2: Choose Your Investments

Beginner-friendly dividend investments:

  • SCHD - Schwab US Dividend Equity ETF (~3.5% yield)
  • VYM - Vanguard High Dividend Yield ETF (~3% yield)
  • DGRO - iShares Dividend Growth ETF (~2.5% yield)
  • Research one of these funds
Week 3: Make Your First Purchase
  • Transfer money to brokerage ($100+)
  • Search for SCHD, VYM, or DGRO
  • Buy your first shares!
  • Enable DRIP (Dividend Reinvestment)
Week 4: Calculate & Automate
  • Calculate your expected annual dividends
  • Set up automatic monthly investment
  • Track when your first dividend will be paid!
üí° Example: $1,000 invested in SCHD (3.5% yield) = ~$35/year in dividends. That's $35 just for owning shares! Now imagine $100,000 = $3,500/year passive income.
üéâ Success = You own dividend-paying investments and have DRIP enabled. You'll receive your first dividend payment within 3 months!
