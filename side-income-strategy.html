üìã Action Plan: Set Up Your System

1 Identify Your Current Priority

Where are you in the waterfall?

  • Do I have $1,000 in emergency savings? If NO ‚Üí Priority 1
  • Do I have high-interest debt (18%+)? If YES ‚Üí Priority 2
  • Do I have 3-6 months expenses saved? If NO ‚Üí Priority 3
  • If all above are done ‚Üí Priority 4 (Wealth building!)
üìç My current priority is: _______________

2 Open a Dedicated Account

Keep side income separate so you don't accidentally spend it:

  • Open a High-Yield Savings Account (HYSA) if you don't have one
  • Name it after your current priority ("Emergency Fund" or "Debt Crusher")
  • Link it to your checking for easy transfers

3 Create Your Income Routing Rule

Write down your rule and stick to it:

üìù My Rule:
"Every time I receive side income, I will immediately transfer ___% to my _____________ account."

Recommended: 100% until your current priority is complete!
  • I've written down my income routing rule
  • I've committed to following it every time

4 Set Up Instant Transfers

When side income hits your account, transfer it IMMEDIATELY:

  • Same day: Don't let it sit in checking overnight
  • Before you check the balance: Transfer first, then look
  • Make it automatic: Some apps (like Venmo) can auto-transfer to your bank
  • I know how to quickly transfer between my accounts
  • I've set up quick transfer shortcuts on my phone

5 Track Every Dollar

Keep a log of all side income and where it went:

  • Create a simple spreadsheet or note: Date | Source | Amount | Where It Went
  • Review monthly to see your progress
  • Celebrate milestones! ($500 saved, first debt paid off, etc.)
