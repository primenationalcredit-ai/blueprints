ğŸ’¡ Pro Tips

  • Personalize proposals: Never copy-paste. Mention their business name and specific needs.
  • Over-communicate: Updates, questions, progress reports. Silence scares clients.
  • Specialize ASAP: "Real Estate VA" pays more than "Virtual Assistant"
  • Get testimonials: After every project, ask for written testimonial.
  • Create systems: Document your processes. Efficiency = more clients.
  • Offer retainers: Monthly packages create predictable income.

âš ï¸ Common Mistakes

  • Racing to the bottom: Competing on price alone attracts bad clients.
  • Scope creep: Define deliverables clearly. Extra work = extra pay.
  • No contracts: Always have written agreement. Protect yourself.
  • Poor time tracking: Use Toggl or similar. Bill accurately.
  • Overcommitting: Taking too many clients = burnout + poor quality.
