Why This Works

When you get new card numbers, EVERY saved card on EVERY website and app stops working. This forces you to manually re-enter your card for each purchase, adding friction that prevents impulse buying. Most people abandon their cart when they have to get up and find their wallet.

The Script - What to Say

When You Call:

"Hi, I'd like to request a new card number for security reasons. I believe my card information may have been compromised."

If They Ask Questions:

"I've noticed some suspicious activity on websites where my card was saved. I'd feel more secure with a fresh card number."

"I appreciate that, but I'd prefer a completely new card number. Can you please process that request?"

Step 1: Call Your Card Companies

  • Primary credit card - Call the number on the back
  • Secondary credit card (if you have one)
  • Store credit cards (Target, Amazon, etc.)
  • Debit card (optional but recommended)

  • Keep new cards at home, not in your wallet
