üí° Business Building Tips

  • Take amazing photos: Every walk, every visit. Happy pet pics = referrals
  • Detailed updates: "Buddy had a great walk! We saw a squirrel and he was SO good!"
  • Remember details: Pet's favorite toy, treat preferences, quirks. Personal touch wins.
  • Flexibility wins: Last-minute requests = premium rates + loyal clients
  • Ask for referrals: "Know anyone else who needs a walker/sitter?"

  • Insurance is essential: $300/year protects you from $10K+ liability
  • Know your limits: Don't board aggressive dogs or more than you can handle
  • Separate pet zones: If boarding, keep client pets separate unless approved
  • Emergency contacts: Always have vet info and owner's emergency contact
  • Contracts: For direct clients, use written agreements
