Why This Works

Most people check their money once a month (or less). By then, problems have snowballed. The Weekly Money Pulse catches issues early when they're easy to fix.

The Rule: Every Sunday at 7pm (or pick your own time). Set a recurring reminder on your phone RIGHT NOW.

The 5-Minute Routine

30 sec

Check Bank Balance

Open your bank app. Note the number. That's it.

2 min

Review This Week's Spending

Scroll through transactions. Any surprises? Any regrets?

1 min

What Worked? What Didn't?

Quick mental note. No judgment, just awareness.

1 min

Set Next Week's Focus

Pick ONE thing to improve. Just one.

30 sec

Text Your Accountability Partner

Quick update. Keep each other honest.
