What Are Sinking Funds?

Sinking funds are savings accounts for planned expenses. Christmas comes every year - it's not an emergency. Car repairs happen - not an emergency. Save a little each month so these expenses are covered.

The Magic: When you KNOW you have $800 saved for Christmas, December isn't stressful. When you KNOW you have $1,500 for car repairs, a flat tire is just an inconvenience, not a crisis.
