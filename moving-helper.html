üìÖ Peak Seasons

When Demand (and Pay) Is Highest

  • End of month: Leases end, everyone moves. Last weekend = CRAZY busy.
  • Summer (May-Sept): Peak moving season. Families move before school.
  • Weekends: Most moves happen Sat-Sun. Book out in advance.
  • 1st of month: New leases start. High demand first few days.
  • College move-in/out: August and May near universities. Massive demand.

üí° Pro Tips

  • Protect the furniture: Use blankets if available. Careful = good reviews.
  • Communicate: "Where do you want this?" Ask before placing items.
  • Work efficiently: Plan your trips. Don't waste time walking empty-handed.
  • Bring your own water: Stay hydrated. This is hard work.
  • Dress for work: Closed-toe shoes, clothes you can move in.
  • Be friendly: Chat, be personable. Happy customers tip more.

‚ö†Ô∏è Important Warnings

  • Lift properly: Bend at knees, not back. Back injuries are serious.
  • Know your limits: Ask for help with heavy items. Don't be a hero.
  • Watch for hazards: Stairs, tight corners, low ceilings. Go slow.
  • Protect yourself: Gloves prevent blisters and cuts.
  • Stay hydrated: Heat exhaustion is real. Take breaks.
