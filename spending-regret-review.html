üí° Pro Tips

üéØ Make Reviews Effective

  • No shame, just data - This is about awareness, not beating yourself up
  • Include everything - Coffee, snacks, small purchases add up
  • Track the "almost bought" - What did you want but didn't buy?
  • Celebrate wins - Note when you resisted temptation
  • Share with accountability partner - Weekly check-in amplifies results

‚ö†Ô∏è Common Pitfalls

  • Don't be too harsh - Some spending is okay; look for patterns, not perfection
  • Don't skip weeks - Consistency matters more than depth
  • Don't just review, act - Create rules from your patterns
  • Don't compare to others - Your journey is unique
