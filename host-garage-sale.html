üí° Pro Pricing Tips

  • Bundle items - "All books $5" or "Fill a bag for $10"
  • Price to sell - Lower prices = more sales = less to donate/trash
  • Mark prices clearly - Use bright stickers so buyers don't have to ask
  • Be ready to negotiate - Price slightly higher knowing people will haggle

üì£ Advertising That Works

1

Facebook Marketplace (FREE - Most Effective)

  • Post 3-5 days before your sale
  • Include 10+ photos of best items
  • List specific items: "Furniture, baby gear, tools, vintage items"
  • Include exact address and date/time
  • Repost the day before
2

Craigslist (FREE)

  • Post in "Garage Sales" section
  • Include neighborhood name in title
  • List specific high-value items to attract buyers
3

Nextdoor App (FREE)

  • Neighbors are most likely to come
  • Post in "For Sale & Free" section
  • Great for furniture (no long-distance pickup)
4

Street Signs (Most Important!)

  • Make signs BIG with thick black marker
  • Just write "GARAGE SALE ‚Üí" with arrow
  • Place at ALL major intersections leading to your home
  • Put signs out morning of sale, remove after

‚ö†Ô∏è Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Don't start too late - Early birds come at 7-8am, serious buyers shop early
  • Don't overprice - Items priced too high won't sell, and you'll end up donating anyway
  • Don't forget change - Everyone pays with $20 bills
  • Don't skip signs - Signs bring 50%+ of buyers who aren't looking online
  • Don't put items in boxes - Display everything where it can be seen
