Storage demand stays strong in recessions. People downsize homes but keep their stuff. Life events (divorce, death, moving) drive demand regardless of economy. Storage held up well in 2008 and 2020!

üìà Storage REITs

Buy shares of Public Storage, Extra Space, CubeSmart. Start with $100. Dividends + appreciation. Most passive option!

ü§ù Syndications

Pool money with other investors to buy facilities. $25K-50K minimum. Professional operators do the work. Need accreditation.

üèóÔ∏è Buy Small Facility

üî® Build or Convert

Step 1: Learn the Storage Industry

‚úì
Step 2: Determine Your Investment Path
Based on capital and involvement level: REITs ($100+, passive), syndications ($25K+, passive), or direct ownership ($200K+, active). Each has tradeoffs!
‚úì
Step 3: Start with REITs (Optional)
Even if planning direct ownership later, invest in storage REITs to learn the industry. Public Storage (PSA), Extra Space (EXR), CubeSmart (CUBE). Track their earnings calls!
‚úì

Join Self Storage Association. Attend industry conferences. Connect with brokers specializing in storage. Deals often come through relationships!
‚úì

Learn to evaluate facilities: occupancy, rental rates vs. market, expense ratios, deferred maintenance. Use broker packages but verify everything. Due diligence is critical!
‚úì

Storage qualifies for SBA loans, commercial mortgages, and seller financing. Banks like storage because of stable cash flow. Get pre-qualified before making offers!
‚úì

Implement revenue management (dynamic pricing). Add online rentals. Reduce costs with automation. Small improvements compound to big returns!
‚úì
Step 8: Scale Your Portfolio
Use cash flow and equity from first facility to acquire more. Many successful operators own 5-20+ facilities. Systems and relationships enable scaling!

üí° Pro Tips & Warnings

üí° Value-Add = Best Returns

Buy underperforming facilities and improve them. Raise below-market rents, improve marketing, add technology. A facility at 70% occupancy with low rents has massive upside vs. stabilized 95% facility!

üí° Technology Enables Passive

Modern storage can run almost unmanned. Online rentals, smart locks, automated payments, remote monitoring. A well-automated facility needs just a few hours weekly!

‚ö†Ô∏è Watch for Oversupply

Some markets have too much new storage being built. Research supply/demand in any market before investing. 7+ square feet per capita suggests potential oversupply. Verify with local market data!
