üí∞ Multi-App Strategy

üéØ How to Stack Apps Like a Pro

  • Run 2-3 apps simultaneously: DoorDash + Uber Eats + Grubhub is the classic stack
  • Cherry-pick orders: Only accept $1.50+/mile minimum. Decline low-ball offers.
  • Accept one, pause others: When you accept, immediately pause other apps
  • Stack same-direction orders: Advanced move‚Äîtwo pickups going same direction
  • Know restaurant speeds: Fast restaurants = more deliveries per hour
  • Decline $3-4 no-tip orders: These kill your hourly. Let someone else take them.

üí° Pro Driver Tips

  • $2/mile rule: Don't accept orders under $1.50-2/mile. Time is money.
  • Hot spots are lies: Apps show "busy areas" but stay where YOU know is busy
  • Position near clusters: Park between 3-4 popular restaurants, not at one
  • Avoid long waits: If restaurant says 15+ min, unassign and move on
  • Text updates: "On my way!" and "At your door!" = better ratings + tips

‚ö†Ô∏è Avoid These Mistakes

  • Accepting every order: Low-ball orders ($3-4) destroy your hourly rate
  • Driving to surge zones: By the time you get there, it's gone. Be IN the zone.
  • Ignoring expenses: Track gas, maintenance, depreciation. Know your TRUE profit.
  • Forgetting taxes: Save 25-30%. You're self-employed!
  • Using one app: Multi-apping is how top earners make $25+/hour
