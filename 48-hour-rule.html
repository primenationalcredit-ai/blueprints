üéØ What This Does For You

Here's what happens without this rule: You see something, you want it, you buy it. The whole process takes 30 seconds. Then a week later you realize you didn't actually need it, but the money's gone.

With the 48-hour rule: You see something, you want it, you wait 48 hours. By then, 70% of the time you've completely forgotten about it or realized you don't actually need it. The other 30%? Those are probably things worth buying!

$200-500+ Saved Monthly

That's $2,400-6,000 per year you keep instead of wasting on impulse buys!

üß† The Science Behind 48 Hours

Why 48 Hours Is the Magic Number

Dopamine fades: When you see something you want, your brain releases dopamine (the "feel good" chemical). This fades within 24-48 hours.

Logical brain takes over: After the dopamine high passes, you can think clearly about whether you actually need the item.

Urgency disappears: That "I NEED this NOW" feeling? It's temporary. In 48 hours, you'll wonder why you felt so urgent.
