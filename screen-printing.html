Step 1: Learn screen printing basics

Step 2: Set up your print shop
Garage or basement works! Get starter press, screens, exposure unit, and heat source for curing. Budget: $1,500-4,000 for basic setup.
‚úì
Step 3: Practice on cheap blanks

Step 4: Source quality blank apparel
Set up wholesale accounts with SanMar, S&S Activewear. Gildan for budget, Bella+Canvas for quality. Blank cost: $2-5 per shirt typically.
‚úì
Step 5: Find your first customers
Start local! Youth sports teams are gold‚Äîthey reorder every season. Schools, churches, small businesses. Offer competitive pricing to build portfolio!
‚úì
Step 6: Deliver quality prints on time

‚úì
Step 7: Get referrals and reviews
