üìã Your Photography Business Action Plan

‚úì
Step 1: Master your camera and editing
Learn manual mode (aperture, shutter, ISO), natural light, composition. Master Lightroom basics. YouTube has free tutorials - Peter McKinnon, Jessica Kobeissi are great!
‚úì
Step 2: Build your portfolio
Shoot friends and family for free or cheap. Do TFP (trade for prints) with aspiring models. Offer mini sessions at low rates. Need 20-30 strong images to start booking!
‚úì
Step 3: Choose your niche
Specialize! Portraits, families, weddings, products, real estate. Specialists book more and charge more. Pick what you love shooting!
‚úì
Step 4: Set your pricing and packages
Research local rates. Create 2-3 clear packages. Include session time, number of edited images, delivery timeline. Start slightly below market, raise as you build reviews.
‚úì
Step 5: Set up your online presence
Instagram portfolio (essential!), Google Business Profile, Facebook page. Simple website optional but helpful. Show your best work - quality over quantity!
‚úì
Step 6: Book your first paying clients
Start pricing low, deliver amazing work. Friends and family count! Post on local groups. Every happy client = referrals. Reviews are everything!
‚úì
Step 7: Deliver consistently and collect reviews
Fast turnaround, beautiful edits, exceed expectations. Ask every client for reviews on Google/Facebook. Reviews build trust and bookings!
‚úì
Step 8: Raise prices and scale
As you book up, raise prices. Add products (prints, albums). Partner with venues. Expand services as demand grows. Successful photographers charge $300-1,000+/session!
