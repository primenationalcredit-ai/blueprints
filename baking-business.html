üí° The Profit Formula

Rule of 3x: Your selling price should be at least 3x your ingredient cost. Decorated cookie costs $0.75 to make? Sell for $3-5 minimum. Factor in your TIME - that's where the real value is!

‚öñÔ∏è Cottage Food Laws

‚ö†Ô∏è Check Your State's Laws First!

Cottage food laws allow home baking with restrictions. Most states allow baked goods, candies, jams. Often has annual sales limits ($25K-75K). May require food handler's permit. Some items restricted (cream fillings, cheesecake). Labeling requirements vary.

Search: "[Your State] cottage food laws" to find your specific requirements!
