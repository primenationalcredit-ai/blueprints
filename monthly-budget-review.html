üéØ Why Do a Monthly Review?

A budget without review is like a GPS without checking if you're on the right road. The monthly review helps you:

  • Catch problems early - Before they become disasters
  • Celebrate wins - See your progress!
  • Adjust your plan - Life changes, your budget should too
  • Stay motivated - Regular check-ins keep you engaged

üìÜ When To Do It

Schedule your monthly review for the 1st or 2nd of each month. Put it in your calendar as a recurring event. Treat it like an important meeting with yourself!
