Step 3: Log into each account and enable autopay

Use checking account for most bills. Use a rewards credit card for bills that don't charge a fee (then autopay the credit card!).

‚úì

Even with autopay, review your bills monthly to catch errors or price increases. Set a monthly calendar reminder.
