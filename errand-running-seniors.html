ğŸš¨ What You Should NOT Do

  • Handle finances directly: Don't manage money, pay bills, or access accounts. Banking assistance means driving them there.
  • Administer medication: Picking up prescriptions is fine. Giving medication requires certification.
  • Medical care: You're not a nurse. No wound care, injections, or medical procedures.
  • Heavy lifting/transfers: Moving furniture or lifting seniors requires training and insurance.
  • Legal documents: Never witness wills or handle legal/financial paperwork.
