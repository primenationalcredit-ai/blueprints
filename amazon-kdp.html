üìã Your KDP Publishing Action Plan

‚úì
Step 1: Create free KDP account
Go to kdp.amazon.com and sign up - completely FREE! You'll need tax info (W-9 for US) for royalty payments. Takes about 15 minutes.
‚úì
Step 2: Research profitable niches
Look for niches with demand but not oversaturated. Use Amazon search, Book Bolt, or Publisher Rocket. "Prayer journal for nurses" beats "journal"!
‚úì
Step 3: Design your interior pages
Use Canva (free) or Book Bolt ($10/mo). Create lined pages, prompts, trackers - whatever fits your book type. Download as PDF. 1-3 hours per book.
‚úì
Step 4: Create eye-catching cover
Cover sells the book! Use Canva templates, Book Bolt, or KDP's free cover creator. Make it pop at thumbnail size. 30-60 minutes per cover.
‚úì
Step 5: Upload and optimize listing
Upload to KDP, write keyword-rich title and description, choose 7 keywords, select categories carefully. Price typically $6.99-$12.99.
‚úì
Step 6: Publish and wait for approval
Hit publish! Book goes live in 24-72 hours. No approval needed for most low-content books. Start your next book while waiting!
‚úì
Step 7: Scale your catalog
One book = lottery ticket. 50+ books = real income. Create variations, explore new niches. Aim for 5-10 new books per month!
‚úì
Step 8: Run Amazon ads (optional)
Amazon Ads can boost visibility. Start with $5/day, target your keywords. Let winners run, kill losers fast. Ads amplify good books!
