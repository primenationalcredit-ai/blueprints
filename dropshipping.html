üìã Your Action Plan

‚úì
Step 1: Find a winning product niche
Research AliExpress trending, TikTok viral products, Facebook ad spy tools. Find problem-solving products with "wow" factor. $20-100 price range ideal!
‚úì
Step 2: Find reliable suppliers
Order samples first! Test quality, packaging, shipping time. Start with CJ Dropshipping or Spocket for faster delivery than AliExpress.
‚úì
Step 3: Build your Shopify store
Professional design builds trust! Good product photos, compelling descriptions, trust badges, clear shipping info. Don't look "dropship-y"!
‚úì
Step 4: Set up payment and shipping
Shopify Payments or Stripe. Set realistic shipping expectations. Be transparent! Bad shipping experience = chargebacks.
‚úì
Step 5: Test ads with small budget
Start with $20-50/day on Facebook or TikTok. Test multiple creatives. Video ads convert best! Watch metrics: CPC, CTR, conversion rate.
‚úì
Step 6: Analyze and optimize
Which ads work? Which products sell? Kill losers fast, scale winners! If no sales after $100 ad spend on a product, move on.
‚úì
Step 7: Scale winning products
Found a winner? Increase ad budget, expand to new audiences, add upsells/bundles. One winning product can do $10K-100K+/mo!
‚úì
Step 8: Build a real brand
Move from dropship to private label. Hold inventory for faster shipping. Build email list. Create brand, not just store!
