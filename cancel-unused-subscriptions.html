üìã Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to find and cancel every subscription you don't need!

  • Step 1: Check your bank and credit card statements
    Log into your bank account and look at the last 3 months of transactions. Search for recurring charges - these are your subscriptions!

    What to look for:

    • Any charge that repeats monthly (same amount, same company)
    • Charges you don't recognize (Google the company name)
    • Annual charges (check last 12 months too!)
    • Small amounts you might ignore ($2.99, $4.99, etc.)
    ‚è±Ô∏è 15 minutes Easy
  • Step 2: Check your email for "receipt" and "subscription"
    Search your email for words like "receipt," "subscription," "renewal," and "charged." You'll find services you forgot about!

    Email search terms to try:

    • "Your receipt" or "payment received"
    • "subscription" or "membership"
    • "will be charged" or "auto-renewal"
    • "monthly" or "annual plan"
    ‚è±Ô∏è 10 minutes Easy
  • Step 3: Check your phone's app subscriptions
    Your phone may be charging you for apps you don't use. Check both the App Store AND Google Play!

    How to check:

    • iPhone: Settings ‚Üí Your Name ‚Üí Subscriptions
    • Android: Play Store ‚Üí Menu ‚Üí Subscriptions
    • Look for apps you haven't opened in months
    • Free trials that converted to paid!
    ‚è±Ô∏è 5 minutes Easy
  • Step 4: List all subscriptions in the tracker below
    Write down every subscription you found. Seeing them all in one place is eye-opening!
    ‚è±Ô∏è 10 minutes Easy
  • Step 5: Decide: Keep or Cancel?
    For each subscription, ask yourself: "Have I used this in the last 30 days?" If not, it's probably time to cancel.

    Questions to ask yourself:

    • When did I last use this?
    • Would I buy this again today if I didn't have it?
    • Is there a free alternative?
    • Can I share this with family (family plans)?
    • Can I pause instead of cancel?
    ‚è±Ô∏è 10 minutes Easy
  • Step 6: Cancel the subscriptions you don't need
    Go cancel them NOW. Don't wait! Most can be canceled online. Some make you call - do it anyway!

    Cancellation tips:

    • Screenshot confirmation of cancellation
    • If they offer a discount to stay, still cancel (you can always re-subscribe)
    • Set a calendar reminder to check next month that you weren't charged
    • For hard-to-cancel services, try justdelete.me
    ‚è±Ô∏è 15-30 minutes Do it now!
