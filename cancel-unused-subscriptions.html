üîé Email Search Terms

Search your email for these terms:

  • subscription - Find all subscription-related emails
  • renewal - Catch auto-renewal notices
  • receipt - Find payment confirmations
  • charged or billing - Billing notifications
  • trial ending - Free trial warnings you missed
  • payment failed - Old subs on expired cards
  • your plan - Plan changes and confirmations
  • monthly or annual - Recurring charge emails
