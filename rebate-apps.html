üìã Your Rebate Action Plan

‚úì
Step 1: Check for rebates BEFORE buying
Research rebates before purchase. Look on manufacturer websites, retailer sites, and Slickdeals. Sometimes rebates are only available through specific stores.
‚úì
Step 2: Read the requirements carefully
Check deadline (usually 30-60 days), required documents (receipt, UPC, serial number), and eligible purchase dates. Missing one requirement = denied rebate.
‚úì
Step 3: Keep ALL packaging and receipts
Don't throw anything away until rebate is approved! You may need UPC codes from boxes, serial numbers, and original receipts.
‚úì
Step 4: Submit immediately after purchase
Don't procrastinate - submit the same day if possible. Deadlines sneak up fast and receipts fade. Take photos of everything as backup.
‚úì
Step 5: Make copies of everything
Photo/scan all documents before submitting. If mail-in, use certified mail with tracking. You need proof if they claim they didn't receive it.
‚úì
Step 6: Get confirmation number
Online submissions give confirmation numbers. Screenshot it! For mail-in, keep the tracking number. You'll need this to check status.
‚úì
Step 7: Set calendar reminder to follow up
Set reminder for 6-8 weeks out. If no rebate received, check status online or call. Companies count on you forgetting!
‚úì
Step 8: Activate and use rebate cards
Many rebates come as prepaid Visa cards. Activate immediately, check expiration, and USE THEM! They often expire in 6-12 months.
