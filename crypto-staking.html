üìö What Is Staking?

üîí Lock Your Crypto

You "stake" (lock up) your coins to help validate transactions on the blockchain network.

üéÅ Earn Rewards

In return, you earn staking rewards - new coins paid to you regularly (daily, weekly, or monthly).

üìà APY = Annual Yield

Staking APY is the annual percentage yield. 5% APY = 5 more coins per 100 staked per year.

‚è∞ Lock-up Periods

Some staking has lock-up periods where you can't withdraw. Others are flexible. Check first!

üí° The Reality Check

Staking rewards are paid in crypto, not dollars. If you stake 1 ETH at 4% APY, you earn 0.04 ETH. But if ETH drops from $2,000 to $1,000, your $2,000 is now worth $1,040 - you lost money despite "earning" rewards! Only stake crypto you're willing to hold long-term.
