üîç How to Identify Metals

üß≤ The Magnet Test

Essential tool!

Steel sticks to magnet. Aluminum, copper, brass DON'T stick. Non-magnetic = more valuable!

üü† Copper

Reddish/orange color

Heavy, soft, bends easily. Turns green when oxidized. MOST valuable common metal!

üü° Brass

Yellow/gold color

Heavy like copper but yellow. Faucets, door hardware, valves, keys, plumbing fittings.

‚ö™ Aluminum

Silver, very light weight

Extremely lightweight - that's the giveaway. Cans, siding, storm doors, lawn furniture.

‚ú® Stainless Steel

Shiny silver, non-magnetic

Doesn't rust, non-magnetic (usually), heavy. Sinks, appliances, grills, pots.

‚¨õ Steel/Iron

Gray, rusts, magnetic

Sticks to magnet! Heavy, rusts. Appliances, car parts, bed frames. Low value but heavy = adds up!
