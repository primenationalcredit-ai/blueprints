üîç Due Diligence Checklist

üìä Verify Traffic

Get Google Analytics access. Check traffic trends (growing/declining?). Verify traffic sources are legit.

üîé Google Search Console

Check for manual penalties, algorithm hits. Look at keyword rankings and click trends.

üíµ Verify Income

Get screenshots of ad earnings (Mediavine, AdSense), affiliate dashboards. Ask for bank statements!

üîó Check Backlinks

Use Ahrefs or SEMrush. Look for spammy links, PBN links, or sudden link spikes (red flags!).

üìà Traffic Trend

Is traffic growing, stable, or declining? Declining traffic = declining income. Be careful!

üèóÔ∏è Content Quality

Is content original, well-written? AI content? Thin content? Quality affects long-term value.

üìã Your Website Flipping Action Plan

‚úì
Step 1: Learn the fundamentals (20-40 hours)
Understand how websites make money (ads, affiliates, products), basic SEO, Google Analytics, and website valuation. Take courses, watch YouTube, read blogs. Don't buy anything yet!
‚úì
Step 2: Browse marketplaces and study valuations
Spend 2-4 weeks browsing Flippa, Motion Invest listings. Study what sites sell for and why. Look at sold listings. Learn to spot red flags. Don't buy yet!
‚úì
Step 3: Set your budget and criteria
Decide how much you can afford to lose (seriously!). Set criteria: site type, niche, traffic source, earnings range. Stay disciplined!
‚úì
Step 4: Do thorough due diligence
When you find a potential site: verify traffic (Google Analytics), verify income (proof!), check backlinks, review content quality, look for red flags. Ask lots of questions!
‚úì
Step 5: Buy your first small site ($500-2,000)
Start small! Your first site is a learning experience. Look for growth potential. Use Escrow.com for safe payment. Expect this might not work out - that's okay!
‚úì
Step 6: Improve the site (3-12 months)
Add quality content, improve SEO, optimize ad placements, improve affiliate offers, fix technical issues. Document everything for future buyers!
‚úì
Step 7: Decide: flip or hold for income
Once improved, you can sell for 2-4x what you paid, or keep it for monthly passive income. Both strategies work!
‚úì
Step 8: Reinvest and scale
Take profits from your flip and buy a bigger site. Or buy multiple small sites. Build a portfolio over time. Learn from each deal!
