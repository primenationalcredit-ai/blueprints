üìã Your YouTube Channel Action Plan

‚úì
Step 1: Choose your niche and audience
Specific niches grow faster! What can you teach or entertain consistently? "Personal finance for millennials" beats "money stuff." Know your viewer avatar.
‚úì
Step 2: Set up your equipment
Start with your phone! Good lighting (window or ring light) and external mic make huge difference. Upgrade as revenue grows.
‚úì
Step 3: Study what works in your niche
Watch top channels in your space. What titles get views? What thumbnails pop? What video length? Don't copy - learn patterns!
‚úì
Step 4: Create your first 10 videos
Hook viewers in first 30 seconds! Value-packed content that solves problems or entertains. Done is better than perfect - you'll improve!
‚úì
Step 5: Master titles and thumbnails
Titles and thumbnails determine if people click! Keyword-rich titles, click-worthy thumbnails. Study what gets views in your niche.
‚úì
Step 6: Upload consistently
1-2 videos/week minimum. Consistency beats perfection. The algorithm rewards regular uploaders. Pick a schedule and STICK to it!
‚úì
Step 7: Build community
Reply to EVERY comment (especially early on). Ask questions in videos. Build relationships with viewers. Engaged audience = everything!
‚úì
Step 8: Monetize multiple ways
Join Partner Program at 1K subs/4K hours. But don't wait - affiliate links work now! Reach out to sponsors, create products, offer services!
