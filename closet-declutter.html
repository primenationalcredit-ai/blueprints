üìã Step-by-Step Guide

1

Empty Everything

Take EVERYTHING out of your closet. Yes, everything. Pile it on your bed.

  • Clothes
  • Shoes
  • Accessories
  • Bags
  • Scarves
2

Sort Into Piles

Create 4 piles: Keep, Sell, Donate, Trash

  • Sell: Items in good condition you don't wear
  • Donate: Worn but usable items
  • Trash: Stained, torn, or unwearable
3

Research What Sells

Check sold listings on Poshmark/Mercari for similar items

4

Take Great Photos

Use natural light, clean background, show any flaws

  • Front view
  • Back view
  • Tag/brand
  • Any defects
5

List on Multiple Platforms

Cross-list to maximize exposure

  • Poshmark (best for brands)
  • Mercari (general)
  • Facebook Marketplace (local)
  • ThredUp (bulk/lazy option)

üí° Pro Tips

  • Check pockets before selling!
  • Wash and iron items for better photos
  • Price 20% higher to leave room for offers
  • Bundle similar items for faster sales
  • Seasonal items sell best at the right time (coats in fall, etc.)
