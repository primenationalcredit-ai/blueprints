üìã Your Podcast Launch Action Plan

‚úì
Step 1: Choose your niche and format
What can you talk about for 100+ episodes? Solo, co-host, or interview format? Weekly or bi-weekly? Pick a specific niche - "business" is too broad, "side hustles for parents" is specific!
‚úì
Step 2: Get basic equipment
Start with your phone! Upgrade to USB mic when ready ($50-100). Audio quality matters more than perfection - just start recording!
‚úì
Step 3: Choose hosting platform
Anchor (Spotify) is free and easy. Buzzsprout has free tier. Your host distributes to all platforms automatically!
‚úì
Step 4: Record your first 3 episodes
Launch with 3+ episodes so new listeners can binge! Don't overthink it - done is better than perfect. You'll improve over time.
‚úì
Step 5: Create cover art and branding
Use Canva (free) for cover art. 3000x3000 pixels. Bold, readable at small sizes. Your face or clear topic imagery.
‚úì
Step 6: Publish and distribute
Submit to Apple Podcasts, Spotify, Google Podcasts. Your host auto-distributes! Write compelling descriptions with keywords.
‚úì
Step 7: Promote and stay consistent
Share on social media, ask for reviews, collaborate with other podcasters. Consistency is KEY - stick to your schedule religiously!
‚úì
Step 8: Monetize as you grow
Start Patreon early (even with 100 fans). Add affiliate links. Reach out to sponsors at 1,000+ downloads/episode. Launch your products!
