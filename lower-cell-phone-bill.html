üìã Your Switching Checklist

‚úì
Step 1: Check if your phone is unlocked
Call your current carrier or check settings. iPhone: Settings ‚Üí General ‚Üí About ‚Üí look for "Carrier Lock." Android: Settings ‚Üí About Phone ‚Üí Status ‚Üí look for SIM status.
‚úì
Step 2: Make sure your phone balance is paid off
If you're still paying off a phone, you usually can't leave until it's paid. Check your bill or call to find out your payoff amount.
‚úì
Step 3: Check coverage in your area
Use the new carrier's coverage map. Since MVNOs use the same towers as big carriers, if Verizon works for you, Visible will too!
‚úì
Step 4: Get your account number and PIN
You'll need these to port (transfer) your number. Call your current carrier or find them in your account online. Write them down!
‚úì
Step 5: Choose your new carrier and plan
Pick based on which network works best in your area. For most people: Mint Mobile (T-Mobile network) or Visible (Verizon network) are best value.
‚úì
Step 6: Order your new SIM (or use eSIM)
Go to your new carrier's website. Select "Keep my number" and enter your account info. Choose physical SIM or eSIM (instant activation).
‚úì
Step 7: Activate your new service
When your SIM arrives (or eSIM downloads), follow the activation instructions. Your number transfers automatically - usually within minutes to a few hours.
‚úì
Step 8: Your old service cancels automatically
Once your number ports, your old carrier automatically closes your account. You may get a final bill for the remaining days of service.
