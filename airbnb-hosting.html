üìã Your Action Plan

‚úì
Step 1: Check local regulations
Some cities require permits or limit short-term rentals. Check your city's rules, lease, and HOA before investing time!
‚úì
Step 2: Prepare your space
Deep clean, declutter, add nice touches. Fresh linens, toiletries, fast WiFi, good lighting. Make it photo-ready!
‚úì
Step 3: Take AMAZING photos
Photos make or break your listing! Natural light, wide angles, show every space. Airbnb sometimes offers free photography.
‚úì
Step 4: Research competitors
Check similar listings in your area. Note their pricing, amenities, reviews. What can you offer that's better?
‚úì
Step 5: Create your listing
Compelling description, accurate amenities, clear house rules. Be honest about what you offer. Detail = trust!
‚úì
Step 6: Set competitive pricing
Start slightly lower to get first reviews. Use Airbnb's smart pricing or PriceLabs. Raise prices as reviews build!
‚úì
Step 7: Install smart lock
Keyless entry makes check-in easy for everyone. $100-200 investment. Generate unique codes per guest. Game changer!
‚úì
Step 8: Deliver 5-star experiences!
Respond fast, be helpful, over-deliver. 5-star reviews = more bookings = higher prices. First few reviews are CRUCIAL!
