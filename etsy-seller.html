üíµ Understanding Etsy Fees

Know Your Costs (Total: ~10-12% per sale)

  • Listing fee: $0.20 per listing (renews every 4 months or when sold)
  • Transaction fee: 6.5% of sale price + shipping
  • Payment processing: 3% + $0.25 per transaction
  • Offsite ads (if enabled): 15% if buyer comes from Etsy ad

Step 1: Choose Your Product Niche

üí° Pro Tips & Warnings

üí° Photos Are Everything

Your first photo is your storefront. Use natural lighting, clean backgrounds, show the product in use. Add a lifestyle photo. Etsy's algorithm favors listings with high click-through rates‚Äîgreat photos get clicks!

üí° Offer Personalization

Personalized items command 20-50% higher prices. "Custom Name Necklace" beats "Gold Necklace." People pay premium for items made just for them. Add personalization options to your listings!
