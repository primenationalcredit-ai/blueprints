3 Set Up the Automatic Transfer

Log into your bank and find "Transfers" or "Move Money":

üè¶ General Steps (Most Banks)

  1. Log into your bank's website or app
  2. Go to "Transfers" or "Move Money"
  3. Select "Set up recurring transfer"
  4. FROM: Checking Account
  5. TO: Savings Account (or HYSA)
  6. Amount: Your calculated amount
  7. Frequency: Match your pay schedule (weekly, bi-weekly, monthly)
  8. Start Date: Day after your next payday
  9. Confirm and save!
