üí° POD Success Tips

  • Simple sells: Text-based designs often outperform complex art
  • Research first: Check bestsellers. What's already selling?
  • Keywords matter: Think like a buyer. What would they search?
  • Evergreen niches: Professions, hobbies sell year-round
  • Volume wins: 100+ designs dramatically increases income

‚ö†Ô∏è Avoid These Mistakes

  • Copyright violation: NO logos, brands, celebrities, sports teams
  • Trademarked phrases: Check USPTO before using phrases
  • Giving up too soon: Takes 3-6 months to see real results
  • Too few designs: 10 designs won't generate significant income
  • Bad keywords: Great design + bad tags = no sales
