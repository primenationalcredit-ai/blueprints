üìã Your House Sitting Action Plan

‚úì
Step 1: Decide your house sitting style
Live-in or check-in? Local only or willing to travel? With pets or homes only? Know what works for your lifestyle!
‚úì
Step 2: Create profiles on platforms
Professional photo, detailed bio, highlight experience with homes/pets. Be specific about what you offer. References are CRUCIAL!
‚úì
Step 3: Start with friends & neighbors
Build experience and references by sitting for people you know. Even free sits build your reputation and get you reviews!
‚úì
Step 4: Get a background check
Some platforms offer this. Having one ready builds instant trust. Well worth the $25-50 investment!
‚úì
Step 5: Apply to listings actively
Be FIRST to apply - speed matters! Personalize each application. Mention specific details about their home/pets. Offer video call!
‚úì
Step 6: Do an AMAZING job
Communicate regularly, send photo updates, leave home cleaner than you found it. 5-star reviews make or break you!
‚úì
Step 7: Ask for reviews & referrals
After every sit, ask for a review. Happy homeowners tell their friends. One great sit = multiple referrals!
‚úì
Step 8: Build repeat clients
Happy homeowners book you again! Build relationships for recurring sits. Premium sitters stay booked year-round!
