üìã How to Run Any No-Spend Challenge

‚úì
Step 1: Choose your challenge level (above)
Be honest with yourself. If you've never done this, start with 24-48 hours. Success breeds success!
‚úì
Step 2: Set your start and end dates
Put it in your calendar. Pick a time without major events, travel, or celebrations.
‚úì
Step 3: Do all necessary shopping BEFORE
Groceries, gas, prescriptions - anything you truly need, get it before your start date.
‚úì
Step 4: Remove temptation
Delete shopping apps, unsubscribe from sale emails, avoid stores and malls, leave cards at home.
‚úì
Step 5: Tell someone for accountability
Text a friend, post on social media, or tell your partner. Accountability dramatically increases success!
‚úì
Step 6: Plan free activities
Boredom leads to spending. Plan walks, library visits, home workouts, calling friends, cooking, cleaning.
‚úì
Step 7: Keep a "want" list
Write down everything you want to buy. After the challenge, review - you'll want most things much less!
‚úì
Step 8: Celebrate and reflect!
Calculate how much you saved. Transfer that amount to savings. Plan your next challenge!
