üìù How to Price Match

‚úì

Step 1: Check Prices Before Shopping

Use apps like Google Shopping, Amazon, or store apps to compare prices before you go. Screenshot lower prices.

‚úì

Step 2: Verify Item is Identical

Check model number, size, color, quantity. Must be EXACT match. "Similar" items don't count.

‚úì

Step 3: Have Proof Ready

Pull up competitor's website/app on your phone. Show the price clearly including the item name and price.

‚úì

Step 4: Ask at Checkout

Politely tell cashier: "I'd like to price match this item." Show your proof. Most will do it instantly.

‚úì

Step 5: Request Manager if Needed

If cashier can't help, politely ask for a manager. They usually have more authority to approve.

‚úì

Step 6: Check Receipts Post-Purchase

Many stores offer 14-30 day price adjustments. If price drops after you buy, get the difference refunded!

üí° Pro Tips

  • Check store's own website: Many stores (Walmart, Target) have lower prices online than in-store
  • Screenshot everything: Prices change fast‚Äîsave proof before checkout
  • Include shipping: If competitor charges shipping, store may not match
  • Stack with coupons: Some stores let you use coupons ON TOP of price match
  • Buy then match: If rushed, buy now and request adjustment within return window

‚ö†Ô∏è What Usually Doesn't Qualify
