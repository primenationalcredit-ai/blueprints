üîç Proofreading vs Editing

üìã Proofreading

Surface-level corrections: Spelling, punctuation, grammar, typos, formatting. The final polish before publication. Easier to learn, lower rates.

‚úèÔ∏è Copy Editing

Line-by-line improvement: Grammar, style, clarity, word choice, sentence structure. More involved than proofreading. Medium rates.

üìù Developmental Editing

Big-picture feedback: Structure, flow, arguments, plot, character. Most skilled, highest rates. Common for books.
