üîê 1. Login Alerts

üí∞ 3. Large Transaction Alerts

üìâ 4. Low Balance Alerts

üèß 8. ATM Withdrawal Alerts

üö® What To Do If You Get a Fraud Alert

‚ö†Ô∏è NEVER Click Links in Alert Messages!

Scammers send fake alerts with links to steal your login. Always open your bank app directly!

If You Get a Suspicious Alert:

1 DON'T click any links in the message

2 Open your bank app directly

3 Check if it's real fraud or something you forgot

4 If fraud: Call the number on the BACK of your card

5 Lock your card in the app

6 Dispute the charge with your bank
