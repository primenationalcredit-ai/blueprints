Step 3: Set up low balance alerts

Step 4: Enable large transaction alerts
Set threshold for transactions over $100 (adjust based on your spending). This catches stolen cards quickly. Include ATM withdrawals!

Step 5: Turn on international/online purchase alerts

âœ“
Step 6: Set up deposit notifications

âœ“
Step 7: Set up the same alerts for credit cards

âœ“
Step 8: Choose delivery method (text + email)

ğŸš¨ What to Do When You Get a Suspicious Alert

Fraud Alert Response (within minutes!):

1. DON'T click links in the alert - could be phishing
2. Open your bank app directly (not from the alert)
3. Check your transactions - is it really fraud?
4. If fraud: Call the bank's fraud number immediately (on back of card)
5. Lock/freeze your card in the app if available
6. Bank will cancel card and issue a new one
7. File a fraud dispute - you're usually not liable!
