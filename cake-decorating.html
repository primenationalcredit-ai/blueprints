üìã Your Cake Decorating Action Plan

‚úì
Step 1: Master decorating techniques
Learn smooth buttercream, piping techniques, fondant basics, drip cakes. Practice consistently! YouTube is your friend. Take a class if possible. Build skills before taking orders!
‚úì
Step 2: Perfect your signature recipes
Develop reliable cake recipes (vanilla, chocolate, red velvet) and your signature buttercream. Consistency is key! Test on family and friends. Your cake must taste as good as it looks!
‚úì
Step 3: Get proper equipment
Invest in quality: stand mixer, turntable, offset spatulas, piping tips, cake boards. Good tools = better cakes = happier customers!
‚úì
Step 4: Check cottage food laws
Research your state's cottage food laws. Most allow home cake businesses with some restrictions. Know limits, labeling requirements, any permits needed.
‚úì
Step 5: Build your portfolio
Make cakes for friends/family at cost to build photos. Photograph EVERYTHING beautifully! Show variety of styles. Need 15-20 great cake photos to start marketing!
‚úì
Step 6: Set up Instagram and online presence
Instagram is essential! Post regularly, use local hashtags (#[City]Cakes #[City]Baker). Create Facebook page. List on The Knot if doing weddings. Consistent branding!
‚úì
Step 7: Book your first paid orders
Start with birthday cakes - lower pressure than weddings. Price fairly (don't undercharge!). Every cake = portfolio photos + potential referrals. Exceed expectations!
‚úì
Step 8: Level up to wedding cakes
Wedding cakes = premium pricing! Connect with planners, list on wedding sites. Offer tastings. Wedding season (May-Oct) can be $5,000-10,000+/month for established decorators!
