üí° Pro Tips

üéØ Make It Stick

  • Use a separate email for shopping accounts so promos never hit your main inbox
  • Install browser extension like "Unsubscriber" to block future sign-ups
  • Use Focus Mode on iPhone/Android to block app notifications during certain hours
  • Set a shopping schedule - only check deals on specific days (like Sunday)
  • Leave retailer loyalty programs - they're notification factories

‚ö†Ô∏è Watch Out For

  • Re-enrollment tricks - Apps may ask to re-enable after updates
  • Essential vs. promo - Keep order/shipping notifications, disable marketing
  • FOMO guilt - You won't actually miss anything important
  • Partner's notifications - Ask them to silence theirs too so you don't hear them
