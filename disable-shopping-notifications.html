üìã How to Disable Notifications

1 iPhone Instructions

üçé For iPhone/iPad:

  1. Open Settings
  2. Scroll down and tap Notifications
  3. Find the app (e.g., Amazon)
  4. Tap on the app name
  5. Toggle OFF "Allow Notifications"
  6. Repeat for each shopping/food app
  • Disabled notifications for Amazon
  • Disabled notifications for Target
  • Disabled notifications for Walmart
  • Disabled notifications for DoorDash
  • Disabled notifications for UberEats
  • Disabled all other shopping app notifications

2 Android Instructions

ü§ñ For Android:

  1. Open Settings
  2. Tap Apps & notifications (or just "Apps")
  3. Tap See all apps
  4. Find and tap the app (e.g., Amazon)
  5. Tap Notifications
  6. Toggle OFF "All notifications"
  7. Repeat for each shopping/food app
  • Completed Android notification settings

3 Disable Email Notifications Too

Many apps also send emails. Go into each app's settings and turn off promotional emails:

  • Disabled promotional emails in Amazon app settings
  • Disabled promotional emails in other shopping apps
  • Unsubscribed from store email lists

üí° Pro Tips

  • Better yet - delete the apps entirely! You can always reinstall if you truly need them
  • Use browser instead of apps - websites can't send push notifications as easily
  • Check notification settings weekly - apps sometimes reset after updates
