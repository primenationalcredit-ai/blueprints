üéØ What Is Plasma Donation?

Plasma is the liquid part of your blood! It's used to make life-saving medicines for people with immune disorders, burns, and bleeding disorders. Your body replaces it within 24-48 hours!

Unlike whole blood donation, you can donate plasma twice per week and get paid each time.

üìã Your Action Plan

Step 1: Find Centers Near You

Google "plasma donation near me" or check the websites above. Most are in shopping centers.

Step 2: Make an Appointment

Most centers let you book online. First visit takes 2-3 hours (includes health screening).

Step 3: Prepare the Night Before

This is important for passing the screening!

  • Drink LOTS of water (at least 8 glasses)
  • Eat protein-rich meal (eggs, chicken, beans)
  • Get good sleep (7-8 hours)
  • Avoid fatty foods and alcohol

Step 4: Bring Required Documents

  • Photo ID (driver's license or state ID)
  • Proof of address (utility bill, bank statement)
  • Social Security card or W-2

Step 5: Complete Your First Donation

The actual donation takes 45-90 minutes. Bring a book, phone, or headphones!

Step 6: Schedule Your Regular Visits

You can donate up to 2x per week with at least 1 day between. Most people go every 3-4 days.

üí° Strategy: Pick the same days each week (like Tue/Sat) to build a routine!
