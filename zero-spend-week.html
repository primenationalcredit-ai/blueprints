Step 2: Do a thorough grocery shop BEFORE
Stock up on everything you need for the full week. Plan meals in advance. This is your one allowed shopping trip!
‚úì
Step 3: Fill your car with gas
If you drive, fill up before the week starts. No gas station trips = no temptation to buy snacks!
‚úì
Step 4: Delete or log out of shopping apps

‚úì
Step 5: Plan 7 days of FREE activities

‚úì
Step 6: Prepare a "temptation list"
