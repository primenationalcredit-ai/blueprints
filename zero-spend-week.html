üìã Preparation Steps (Do These BEFORE Your Week Starts!)

‚úì
Step 1: Pick Your Week and COMMIT!
Choose a week without major events. Write the dates down!
üìÖ How to Choose Your Week:
  • Look at the next 2-3 weeks on your calendar
  • Avoid weeks with: Birthdays, holidays, travel, social events you can't skip
  • Good weeks: "Normal" weeks with work/school and not much else
  • Write it down NOW: "My zero-spend week is: ______ to ______"
  • Put it in your calendar - Block off the whole week!
  • Tell someone TODAY: Text a friend or family member right now. Accountability = success!
‚úì
Step 2: Do a THOROUGH Grocery Shop (Before Your Week!)
Stock up on EVERYTHING you'll need for 7 full days. This is the key to success!
üõí Your Pre-Challenge Shopping List:
  • Breakfasts for 7 days: Eggs, bread, cereal, oatmeal, fruit, yogurt, whatever you eat
  • Lunches for 7 days: Sandwich stuff, salad ingredients, leftovers containers, soups
  • Dinners for 7 days: Plan 7 specific meals! Write them down! Buy all ingredients!
  • Snacks: Chips, fruit, nuts, popcorn, crackers, whatever keeps you from wanting to buy snacks
  • Drinks: Coffee, tea, juice, sodas - whatever you normally buy at convenience stores
  • Treats: Ice cream, cookies, candy - yes, buy these! It's easier to resist going out when you have treats at home
  • Toiletries: Check if you need toothpaste, toilet paper, shampoo - stock up now!
  • Do this shopping 1-2 days before your week starts!
‚úì
Step 3: Fill Your Car with Gas
If you drive, fill up your tank BEFORE the week starts!
‚õΩ Why This Matters:
  • Gas stations are FULL of temptations: Snacks, drinks, lottery, impulse buys
  • If your tank is full, you won't need to stop at a gas station all week
  • No gas station = no temptation to grab "just a drink" or "just a snack"
  • Fill up completely the day before or morning of Day 1
  • If you don't drive: Great! Skip this step!
‚úì
Step 4: Delete or Log Out of ALL Shopping Apps
Remove the temptation completely! No apps = no impulse buying.
üì± Apps to Delete Right Now:
  • Amazon - Press and hold ‚Üí Delete. #1 impulse buying app!
  • Target - Delete it!
  • Walmart - Gone!
  • DoorDash, UberEats, Grubhub - ALL food delivery apps, delete them!
  • Instacart - No grocery delivery either!
  • Any other shopping apps - Nike, Shein, Wayfair, etc.
  • Can't delete? At least LOG OUT so you can't one-click buy
  • Remember: You can re-download them after the week. This is temporary!
‚úì
Step 5: Plan 7 Days of FREE Activities
Boredom leads to spending! Fill your week with fun things that cost $0.
üéâ Free Activities to Plan:
  • Outdoor: Walks, hikes, park visits, bike rides, sitting outside to read
  • Library: Borrow books, movies, audiobooks. Many have free events too!
  • Exercise: Home workouts (YouTube), yoga, running, bodyweight exercises
  • Social: Call friends/family, host a potluck (everyone brings food!), game night with stuff you own
  • Creative: Cook new recipes, organize/declutter, journal, DIY projects
  • Entertainment: Shows you already pay for, games you already own, books you already have
  • Write down at least 1-2 activities for each day!
‚úì
Step 6: Hide Your Wallet and Cards
Make spending INCONVENIENT. If your card isn't easy to grab, you won't use it!
üí≥ Remove the Temptation:
  • Take all cards out of your wallet - Put them in a drawer, shoebox, or give to a friend
  • Remove cash too - Put it in the same hiding spot
  • Keep only your ID - In case of emergencies
  • Disable Apple Pay / Google Pay - Remove cards from your phone wallet
  • The goal: Make it HARD to spend. If there's a true emergency, you can get your cards. But impulse purchases? Impossible!

üí° Survival Tips for Each Day

üß† When You Feel the Urge to Spend STOP ‚Üí Take a breath ‚Üí Write down what you wanted and why ‚Üí Wait 10 minutes ‚Üí The urge usually passes! If not, distract yourself with a free activity.

üíß Drink Water First Sometimes we think we're bored, stressed, or need to buy something - but we're actually just thirsty or hungry. Drink a big glass of water, eat a snack you already have, then see if the urge is still there.

üèÉ Change Your Environment Feeling stuck? Go outside. Walk around the block. Move to a different room. Changing your physical space changes your mental state and breaks the "I need to buy something" feeling.

üìù Keep a "Temptation List" Every time you want to buy something, write it down instead of buying it. After the week, review the list. How many things do you still actually want? Most will seem silly!

üîÑ If You Slip Up... DON'T QUIT! If you accidentally spend, don't use it as an excuse to give up. Just continue with the remaining days. Some zero-spend days are better than none!
