Preparation Checklist (Do BEFORE You Start)

  • Meal prep enough food for the full week
  • Fill up your gas tank
  • Delete shopping apps from your phone
  • Leave credit cards at home
  • Tell your accountability partner
  • Unsubscribe from promotional emails
  • Plan free activities (walks, library, home workouts)

Daily Survival Checklist

  • Morning: Pack lunch, snacks, and water bottle
  • Temptation hits: Text your accountability partner instead
  • Evening: Write down any urges you resisted
  • Before bed: Celebrate another $0 day!
‚ö†Ô∏è If You Slip: Don't give up! Write down what happened, why, and restart the next day. Progress over perfection!
üí° Pro Tip: Schedule your Zero-Spend Week for the week AFTER payday. You'll have groceries stocked and bills paid, making it easier to succeed.

After the Challenge

  • Calculate how much you saved
  • Transfer that amount to your savings account
  • Schedule your next Zero-Spend Week (1 per month recommended)
  • Identify 2-3 habits you want to keep permanently
