üìã Before You Call - Preparation

‚úì
Step 1: Have your account info ready
Account number, current plan, current monthly cost. Have a recent bill in front of you.
‚úì
Step 2: Research competitor prices
Look up what competitors charge for similar service. This is your leverage! "Competitor X is offering..."
‚úì
Step 3: Know your customer history
How long have you been a customer? Do you pay on time? Loyal customers have more leverage.
‚úì
Step 4: Be ready to be transferred
You may need to ask for the "retention" or "loyalty" department. They have more power to offer discounts.

üìû Script 1: Internet/Cable Bill

üìù Copy & Use This Script

YOU:
"Hi, I've been a customer for [X years] and I've noticed my bill has increased. I really like your service, but I've been looking at other options because the price is getting too high for my budget. Can you help me find a better rate?"
üí° Wait for their response. They'll often offer something right away.
IF THEY SAY NO:
"I understand. Could you transfer me to your retention or loyalty department? I'd really like to stay with [Company], but I need to find a way to lower this bill."
RETENTION TEAM:
"I've been a loyal customer for [X years] and always pay on time. I've seen that [Competitor] is offering [specific deal]. I'd prefer to stay with you, but I need my bill closer to [$XX/month]. What can you do for me?"
üí° Be willing to walk away. Sometimes the best deal comes when you say "Let me think about it and call back."

üìû Script 2: Cell Phone Bill

üìù Copy & Use This Script

YOU:
"Hi, I'm reviewing my monthly expenses and my phone bill is higher than I'd like. I've been looking at [Mint Mobile/Visible/competitor] which offers similar service for much less. Before I switch, I wanted to see if there are any promotions or loyalty discounts available for long-term customers like me."
IF THEY CHECK YOUR ACCOUNT:
"I'm currently paying [$XX/month]. I'd really like to get that down to [$XX] or lower. I use about [X GB] of data per month, so I might be on a plan that's bigger than I need. What options do you have?"
üí° Ask about: autopay discounts, loyalty discounts, promotional rates, switching to a smaller plan

üìû Script 3: Insurance (Car/Home)

üìù Copy & Use This Script

YOU:
"Hi, I've been a customer for [X years] with no claims, and I'm shopping around for better rates. Before I switch companies, I wanted to see if there are any discounts I might be missing or if you can offer me a better rate."
ASK ABOUT THESE DISCOUNTS:
"Can you check if I qualify for: bundling discount, safe driver discount, low mileage discount, good credit discount, autopay discount, paperless billing discount, professional organization discounts, alumni discounts?"
IF NEEDED:
"I got a quote from [Competitor] for [$XX less per month]. Can you match or beat that? I'd prefer to stay with you since I've been here so long."
üí° Get 3 quotes from competitors first. This gives you real leverage!
