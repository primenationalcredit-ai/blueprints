üìã Your Negotiation Action Plan

‚úì
Step 1: Gather your current bills
Collect statements for internet, phone, insurance, and any recurring bills. Know exactly what you're paying - this is your baseline.
‚úì
Step 2: Research competitor prices
Spend 10 minutes finding what competitors charge. Screenshot new customer deals. This is your leverage!
‚úì
Step 3: Choose the right time to call
Call Tuesday-Thursday, mid-morning (10am-2pm). End of month is best - reps have quotas. Avoid Mondays and Fridays.
‚úì
Step 4: Ask for the retention department
Regular customer service can't give big discounts. Say "I'm thinking of canceling" to get transferred to retention - they have the real power!
‚úì
Step 5: Use the scripts below
Be polite but firm. Mention loyalty and competitor prices. Use strategic silence after asking for a discount - let them fill the gap!
‚úì
Step 6: Always ask "What else can you do?"
Their first offer is rarely their best. After they give you something, ask this question. Often they'll find another discount!
‚úì
Step 7: Get it in writing
Ask for email confirmation of the new rate. Ask how long the discount lasts. Set a calendar reminder to renegotiate before it expires!
‚úì
Step 8: If they say no, hang up and call back
Different rep = different result. Some reps are more empowered than others. Try 2-3 times before giving up.
