üìã Your Step-by-Step Action Plan

‚úì
Step 1: Gather Your Current Bills
Before you call, know exactly what you're paying. Have this info ready!
üìù What You Need:
  • Your current bill/statement - Know the exact amount you pay
  • How long you've been a customer - "I've been with you for 5 years..."
  • Your account number - Speeds up the call
  • Competitor prices - We'll get these in Step 2!
  • A quiet place to call - No background noise
  • Paper and pen - Take notes during the call!
‚úì
Step 2: Research Competitor Prices (5-10 Minutes)
This is your LEVERAGE! You need real numbers to mention.
üîç How to Research:
  • Google "[competitor] new customer deals" - Screenshot what you find!
  • For internet: Check Xfinity, AT&T, Spectrum prices for new customers
  • For cell: Check Mint Mobile, Visible, T-Mobile prepaid plans
  • For insurance: Get actual quotes from GEICO, Progressive, State Farm (takes 10 min online)
  • Write down: "[Competitor] is offering $X for [service]"
  • Having real numbers gives you POWER in the conversation!
‚úì
Step 3: Ask for the Retention Department!
This is the SECRET! Regular customer service can't give big discounts. Retention can!
üéØ The Magic Words:
  • Say: "I'm thinking about canceling my service"
  • This gets you transferred to the "retention" or "loyalty" department
  • These people have REAL power to give discounts!
  • If they ask why you're canceling: "The price is too high compared to competitors"
  • Don't be rude! Be polite but firm. They want to help you!
‚úì
Step 4: Use the Scripts Below (Read Them Exactly!)
It's OK to read the scripts word-for-word. They work!
üí¨ Script Tips:
  • Have the script open on your phone or computer while you call
  • It's OK to read it! They can't see you
  • Speak slowly and clearly - Don't rush
  • Be friendly! "Thank you so much" and "I really appreciate it"
  • Use strategic silence - After you ask for a discount, STOP TALKING. Let them respond!
‚úì
Step 5: Always Ask "What Else Can You Do?"
Their first offer is NEVER their best! Always push for more.
üéØ The Power Question:
  • After they offer something, say: "I appreciate that! Is there anything else you can do?"
  • Often they'll find ANOTHER discount on top of the first one!
  • Ask at least twice before accepting
  • Also ask: "Are there any other promotions I might qualify for?"
  • You'd be surprised how often they have more they can offer!
‚úì
Step 6: Get It In Writing!
Always get email confirmation of your new rate.
üìß What to Ask For:
  • Say: "Can you please email me confirmation of this new rate?"
  • Get the rep's name and note it down
  • Ask: "How long does this promotional rate last?"
  • Set a calendar reminder for 1 month BEFORE it expires to call again!
  • Check your next bill to make sure the discount is applied
‚úì
Step 7: If They Say No - Hang Up and Call Back!
Different rep = different result! Don't give up after one try.
üîÑ The Call-Back Strategy:
  • Some reps are more empowered (or nicer!) than others
  • Thank them politely and hang up
  • Wait 15-30 minutes and call again
  • You'll get a different person who might help more
  • Try 2-3 times before giving up on any bill
  • Different days work too - Try calling on a different day
