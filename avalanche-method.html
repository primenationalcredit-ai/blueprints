üéØ What This Does For You

The Debt Avalanche is the smartest way to pay off debt. You attack your highest-interest debt first (like credit cards at 24%) before lower-interest debt (like car loans at 7%).

Why? Because high-interest debt grows FAST. A $5,000 credit card at 24% costs you $1,200/year in interest! By killing the highest rate first, you save the most money and get debt-free faster.

Save $500-2,000+ in Interest

Get debt-free months or even YEARS faster than paying randomly!

üìä How the Avalanche Works

The Simple 4-Step Process:

1
List all your debts by interest rate ‚Äî Put the highest rate at the top
2
Pay the minimum on ALL debts ‚Äî This keeps you from getting late fees
3
Throw ALL extra money at the highest-rate debt ‚Äî Every extra dollar attacks the most expensive debt
4
When #1 is paid off, move to #2 ‚Äî Roll that payment to the next highest rate

  • ‚úÖ Mathematically optimal
  • ‚è≥ May take longer to see first payoff
  • üëç Best if you're motivated by math/savings

‚õÑ Snowball (Smallest Balance First)

üí° Bottom Line:
