What is the Avalanche Method?

Pay minimum on all debts, then throw ALL extra money at the debt with the highest interest rate (APR). Once that's paid off, move to the next highest. Repeat until debt-free.

Why It Works: High-interest debt grows fastest. By killing it first, you pay less total interest and get out of debt sooner.
