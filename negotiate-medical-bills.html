üìã Your Medical Bill Action Plan

‚úì
Step 1: Request an itemized bill
Call billing and ask for a DETAILED itemized bill with CPT codes (procedure) and ICD codes (diagnosis). The summary bill isn't enough - you need line items to check for errors.
‚úì
Step 2: Check for billing errors
Review each line item. Look for duplicates, services you didn't receive, incorrect room charges, wrong medication dosages, or "unbundling" (charging separately for bundled items).
‚úì
Step 3: Research fair prices
Use Healthcare Bluebook and FAIR Health to see what procedures typically cost. Medicare pays 40-60% less than "list price" - that's your negotiation target.
‚úì
Step 4: Ask about financial assistance
Ask: "Do you have charity care or financial assistance programs?" Most hospitals are REQUIRED to have these but don't advertise them. You may qualify for 100% forgiveness!
‚úì
Step 5: Call and negotiate
Be polite but firm. Ask for cash-pay discount, hardship reduction, or payment plan. Use the scripts below. If they say no, ask to speak to a supervisor.
‚úì
Step 6: Request a payment plan
If you can't pay in full, request the longest payment plan possible. Many hospitals offer 0% interest plans. Even $25-50/month shows good faith.
‚úì
Step 7: Get everything in writing
BEFORE paying, get the agreement in writing via email or letter. Keep records of all calls including date, time, rep name, and what was agreed.
‚úì
Step 8: Consider Dollar For or professional help
Dollar For is a FREE nonprofit that helps get medical debt forgiven. For large bills, a medical billing advocate (paid service) can often save more than they cost.

üí¨ Negotiation Scripts

üìû Script 1: Ask for Cash-Pay Discount

"Hi, I received a bill for $[amount] for services on [date]. I'd like to pay this bill, but the amount is more than I can afford. If I pay in full today, is there a cash-pay discount or prompt-pay discount available? I've seen that Medicare pays around $[lower amount] for this procedure, so I was hoping we could come to something closer to that."

üìû Script 2: Request Financial Assistance

"I'm calling about bill #[number] for $[amount]. This bill is causing financial hardship for my family. I want to pay what I owe, but I need help. Do you have any financial assistance programs or charity care I might qualify for? I'd be happy to provide income documentation. I want to work something out rather than not pay at all."

üìû Script 3: Request Payment Plan

"I'd like to set up a payment plan for this bill. What's the longest payment plan you offer? Is there any interest charged? I can afford $[amount] per month. Can we make that work? I want to pay this off - I just need some time."

üìû Script 4: Dispute an Error

"I reviewed my itemized bill and I believe there's an error. I see a charge for [item] but I didn't receive that service / that appears to be a duplicate / that's not the correct code. Can you review this and correct the bill? I'll hold while you check, or call me back at [number]."
