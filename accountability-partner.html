How to Ask - The Script

The Opening:

"Hey [Name], I'm working on getting my finances under control and I need some help. Would you be willing to be my accountability partner?"

Explain What It Means:

"All I need is someone to check in with once a week - just a quick text. I'll share my wins and struggles, and it helps me stay on track knowing someone's in my corner."

Make It Easy:

"It's literally just responding to a text once a week. You don't need to give advice or anything - just knowing you're there helps me stay accountable."

Step 1: Choose Your Partner

  • Write down 3 people who might be a good fit
  • Pick the one you feel most comfortable sharing finances with
  • Reach out to them TODAY (don't wait!)

Step 2: Set Up Your Check-In System

  • Agree on a check-in day (Sunday evenings work great)
  • Choose a method: Text, call, or in-person
  • Set a recurring reminder on your phone
  • Share your 90-day financial goal with them
