üìã Your Action Plan

‚úì
Step 1: Explore if coaching is right for you
Read "The Prosperous Coach" by Rich Litvin. Talk to working coaches. Attend a free coaching demo. Make sure this aligns with your calling!
‚úì
Step 2: Choose your specialization
What transformation can YOU help with? Your life experience matters! Career change? Health journey? Business building? Pick your niche.
‚úì
Step 3: Start coaching immediately (even without certification)
Coach friends and family for free. Get practice hours. Learn by doing! You don't need permission to help people.
‚úì
Step 4: Select a certification program
Research ICF-accredited programs. Consider budget, time, learning style. CTI, iPEC, and Life Coach Training Institute are top choices.
‚úì
Step 5: Complete your training
Invest in yourself! Complete coursework, practice hours, and mentor coaching. This is your foundation for success.
‚úì
Step 6: Get your first paying clients
Offer discounted "founding client" rates. Start with $50-100/session. Get testimonials and case studies!
‚úì
Step 7: Apply for ICF credential
Once you have training + coaching hours, apply for ACC (Associate Certified Coach). This opens doors and commands higher rates!
‚úì
Step 8: Build your coaching business
Raise rates, add group programs, create courses. Continue education. Aim for PCC level. Build your dream coaching practice!
