How to Delete Apps

  • iPhone: Press and hold app > Remove App > Delete
  • Android: Press and hold app > Uninstall
  • Go through your home screen AND app library
  • Check folders - apps hide in there!
üí° Pro Tip: If you NEED something from Amazon, use a computer browser. The extra steps (finding laptop, logging in, typing) will make you think twice. Most "needs" turn out to be "wants" when you have to work for it.

After Deleting

  • Turn off "Suggested Apps" in your phone settings
  • Block shopping websites in your browser (optional but powerful)
  • Tell your accountability partner what you deleted
  • Notice how much calmer you feel without notifications
  • Celebrate your commitment to your financial goals!
