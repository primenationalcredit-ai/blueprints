Step 1: Delete the Amazon App
Amazon is the #1 impulse buying app for most people. Let's start with the big one!
üì¶ How to Delete Amazon:
iPhone:
  1. Find the Amazon app on your home screen
  2. Press and HOLD the app icon (don't just tap)
  3. Wait for the menu to appear
  4. Tap "Remove App"
  5. Tap "Delete App"
  6. Tap "Delete" to confirm
  7. DONE! The app is gone!
Android:
  1. Find the Amazon app on your home screen or app drawer
  2. Press and HOLD the app icon
  3. Drag it to "Uninstall" at the top of the screen, OR
  4. Tap "Uninstall" if a menu appears
  5. Tap "OK" to confirm
  6. DONE!

Remember: You can still use Amazon.com in your browser if you really need something. You're just removing the easy one-tap temptation!
