üîç Where to Find Items

Best sourcing spots:

  • Thrift stores - Goodwill, Salvation Army, local thrifts (go often!)
  • Garage/estate sales - Early bird gets the deals, Saturdays are best
  • Facebook Marketplace - Free section, underpriced listings
  • Retail clearance - Target, Walmart, store closeouts
  • Retail arbitrage - Buy clearance at stores, sell on Amazon
  • Auctions - Storage unit auctions, estate auctions
  • Craigslist free section - People give away valuable stuff!
