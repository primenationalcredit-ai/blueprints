Step 1: Check if you're eligible
Age 18-40 preferred (up to 60 for some registries). Generally good health. Some conditions may disqualify you - registries have detailed health questionnaires.
‚úì
Step 2: Join Be The Match registry
Visit bethematch.org and request a free swab kit. They mail it to you. Simple cheek swab, mail it back. You're on the registry!

Gift of Life and DKMS are also free to join. More registries = more chance of being a match for someone in need.
‚úì

If you move or change phone numbers, update your registry profile! They need to reach you if you're a match.
‚úì

‚úì

Additional blood testing, physical exam, then donation. PBSC (80% of cases): injections + blood draw. Marrow extraction (20%): outpatient procedure. All expenses covered!

Travel, lodging, meals, lost wages all covered. Time compensation typically $300-500+. Medical costs 100% covered. You pay nothing!
