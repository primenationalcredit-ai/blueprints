ğŸ’¡ Flip Like a Pro

  • Look for solid wood: Particle board isn't worth flipping
  • Dovetail joints: Sign of quality. Worth more.
  • Trendy colors: White, black, navy, sage sell fastest
  • Before/after photos: Show transformation. Gets more interest.
  • Measure everything: Include dimensions in listing

âš ï¸ Avoid These Mistakes

  • Skip cheap paint: Good paint = better finish = higher price
  • Don't skip sanding: Paint won't stick to glossy surfaces
  • Avoid smoke/pet smell: Very hard to remove. Pass on these.
  • Overpaying: Best pieces are free or under $30
  • Too trendy: Classic colors sell better long-term
