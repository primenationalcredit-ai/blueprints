üìã Your Action Plan

‚úì
Step 1: Find your first piece
Start with a solid wood dresser or nightstand. Look for good bones, ignore surface damage. FB Marketplace "free" section or curbside finds!
‚úì
Step 2: Clean thoroughly
TSP cleaner removes grime. Sand lightly to rough up surface. Remove all hardware. Prep is 80% of the work!
‚úì
Step 3: Prime and paint
Quality primer (Zinsser/Kilz) + 2 coats of paint. Don't cheap out on paint‚Äîbetter paint = better finish = higher price!
‚úì
Step 4: Add new hardware
$10-20 for new knobs/pulls transforms the piece. Gold, black, or brushed nickel are popular. Game changer!
‚úì
Step 5: Stage and photograph
Clean background, natural light, style with decor. Before/after photos get more interest! Photos sell furniture.
‚úì
Step 6: List and price
FB Marketplace, Craigslist, OfferUp. Price 20% above target to allow negotiation. Include dimensions!
‚úì
Step 7: Sell and repeat
Arrange pickup (garage or porch). Reinvest profits into more pieces. Scale up to bigger items!
‚úì
Step 8: Build your pipeline
Always be sourcing! Have multiple pieces in progress. Source on Saturdays, paint on Sundays, sell during the week.

üí° Pro Tips

üî• Flip Like a Pro

Solid wood only: Particle board isn't worth your time. Look for dovetail joints = quality!

Before/after photos: Show the transformation. Gets way more interest than just "after".

Measure everything: Include dimensions in listing. Buyers need to know if it fits!

Skip smoke/pet smell: Very hard to remove. Pass on these pieces.

Best pieces are free: Or under $30. No profit if you overpay for inventory!
