üìã Your Space Rental Action Plan

‚úì
Step 1: Identify your space's appeal
What makes your space unique? Style, architecture, garden, views, size? Think like a location scout - what would someone want to photograph here?
‚úì
Step 2: Check regulations & rules
Review HOA rules, local regulations on commercial use, and homeowner's insurance. Most residential use is fine, but film permits may be needed for large productions.
‚úì
Step 3: Prepare your space
Clean, declutter, stage your space. Remove personal items. Make it "photo-ready." Consider what angles look best and highlight those areas.
‚úì
Step 4: Take professional-quality photos
Great photos are ESSENTIAL. Shoot during golden hour or good light. Multiple angles. Show the whole space and detail shots. Consider hiring a photographer!
‚úì
Step 5: Create listings on platforms
Sign up for Peerspace and Giggster. Write detailed descriptions: size, light, amenities, parking, bathroom, what it's good for. Be specific!
‚úì
Step 6: Set competitive pricing
Research similar spaces in your area. Set hourly rate and minimum hours. Consider different rates for photos vs. film vs. events. Start slightly lower to get reviews!
‚úì
Step 7: Define house rules & access
What areas are available? Off-limits? Parking rules? Noise limits? Equipment restrictions? Clear rules prevent problems!
‚úì
Step 8: Host bookings & build reviews
Respond quickly to inquiries. Be accommodating. Make guests' experience great. Ask for reviews - they're crucial for attracting premium clients!
