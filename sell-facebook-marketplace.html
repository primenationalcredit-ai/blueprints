üìã Step-by-Step: Start Selling

  • Step 1: Walk through your home and gather items
    Check closets, garage, basement, attic. If you haven't used it in a year, it can probably go!

    Look in these spots:

    • Closets (clothes, shoes, bags)
    • Garage (tools, sports equipment, car stuff)
    • Kids' rooms (outgrown toys, clothes, gear)
    • Kitchen (appliances you never use)
    • Basement/attic (furniture, decorations, old electronics)
    ‚è±Ô∏è 1-2 hours Fun treasure hunt!
  • Step 2: Clean items and take GREAT photos
    Good photos = faster sales! Use natural light, clean background, multiple angles.

    Photo tips:

    • Natural daylight is best (near a window)
    • Plain background (white wall, clean floor)
    • Multiple angles (front, back, any flaws)
    • Show scale (put something familiar next to it)
    • Clean the item first!
    ‚è±Ô∏è 5 min/item Key to selling!
  • Step 3: Research prices
    Search Marketplace for similar items. Price 10-20% below similar listings to sell faster.
    ‚è±Ô∏è 2-3 min/item Easy
  • Step 4: Write clear, honest descriptions
    Include: condition, dimensions, brand, why you're selling. Be honest about flaws!

    Good listing template:

    • Title: Brand + Item + Key Feature (e.g., "IKEA MALM Dresser - 6 Drawers - White")
    • Condition: Excellent / Good / Fair
    • Details: Size, color, age, any flaws
    • Why selling: "Moving" or "Upgraded" sounds better than "Getting rid of"
    • Pickup info: Where, when available
    ‚è±Ô∏è 3-5 min/item Easy
  • Step 5: Post your listings!
    Open Facebook > Marketplace > Create Listing. Add photos, description, price, and post!
    ‚è±Ô∏è 2-3 min/item Easy
  • Step 6: Respond to messages FAST
    Quick responses = more sales. First person to show up with cash usually gets it!
    ‚è±Ô∏è Ongoing Key to sales!
  • Step 7: Meet safely, get paid cash
    Meet in public or have someone with you. Cash only (or Venmo before they arrive). Trust your gut!

    Safety tips:

    • Meet in public (police stations often have "safe exchange zones")
    • Bring a friend or tell someone where you'll be
    • Cash or Venmo/PayPal BEFORE handing over the item
    • Daytime meetings only
    • Trust your instincts - cancel if something feels off

üí° Pro Tips for Fast Sales

Listings with 4+ good photos sell 3x faster than listings with 1-2 photos. Take the extra minute!
