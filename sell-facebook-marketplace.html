üìã Your Selling Action Plan

‚úì
Step 1: Walk through your home and gather items
Check closets, garage, basement, attic, kids' rooms. If you haven't used it in a year, it can probably go! Make a pile of everything to sell.
‚úì
Step 2: Clean items and take GREAT photos
Good photos = faster sales! Wipe everything down. Use natural light near a window. Plain background. Take 4+ photos from multiple angles including any flaws.
‚úì
Step 3: Research prices on Marketplace
Search for similar items. See what they're listed for AND what actually sold. Price 10-20% below similar listings to sell faster. The first sale is the best sale!
‚úì
Step 4: Write clear, honest descriptions
Include brand, condition, dimensions, why selling. Be honest about any flaws - buyers appreciate honesty and won't leave bad reviews. Use keywords buyers search for!
‚úì
Step 5: Post your listings
Open Facebook ‚Üí Marketplace ‚Üí Create Listing. Add photos (best one first!), description, price. Post to multiple categories if applicable. List on OfferUp too!
‚úì
Step 6: Respond to messages FAST
Quick responses = more sales! The first person to show up with cash usually gets it. Check messages frequently. Be friendly and accommodating with pickup times.
‚úì
Step 7: Meet safely, get paid cash
Meet in public or have someone with you. Cash only (or Venmo/PayPal BEFORE handing over item). Daytime meetings only. Trust your gut - cancel if something feels off!
‚úì
Step 8: Relist items that don't sell
No bites after a week? Delete and repost to bump to the top. Or lower the price. Bundle slow sellers: "Take all 3 for $50!" moves inventory fast!
