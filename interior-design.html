üìã Your Interior Design Action Plan

‚úì
Step 1: Develop your design skills
Study color theory, space planning, furniture arrangement, lighting. Online courses (Skillshare, Udemy), design books, YouTube tutorials. Practice in your own home and friends' spaces!
‚úì
Step 2: Define your services and pricing
Start with services like color consultations, room refreshes, e-design. Research local rates. Create clear packages. Don't undervalue your eye and expertise!
‚úì
Step 3: Build your toolkit
Measuring tools, color fan decks, design software (Canva for mood boards, SketchUp for floor plans). Good camera or phone for portfolio photos!
‚úì
Step 4: Create your portfolio
Design your own space beautifully. Offer friends/family discounted designs for portfolio photos. Before/after photos are gold! Need 10-15 strong project photos to start.
‚úì
Step 5: Set up your online presence
Instagram (essential!), Pinterest, Houzz profile. Post regularly, use location hashtags. Show your work, share tips, build following. Before/afters perform best!
‚úì
Step 6: Get your first clients
Start with friends, family, neighbors at friendly rates. Post on Nextdoor. Offer e-design to reach more people. Every project builds your portfolio!
‚úì
Step 7: Deliver transformative results
Listen to clients, understand their lifestyle and budget. Present ideas professionally. Manage expectations. Take amazing before/after photos. Ask for reviews!
‚úì
Step 8: Expand and specialize
Raise prices as demand grows. Add home staging (lucrative!). Specialize in a niche (modern, bohemian, family-friendly). Partner with contractors, real estate agents!
