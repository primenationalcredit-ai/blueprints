‚úì

‚úì
Step 3: Create packages and pricing
Offer 2-3 packages based on hours and deliverables. Include hours of coverage, number of edited photos, delivery timeline. Research local rates!
‚úì
Step 4: Set up online presence
Instagram portfolio, Google Business Profile, Facebook page. Join local groups. Thumbtack for leads. Show your best event work!
‚úì
Step 5: Book your first paying events
Start with birthday parties, graduations - lower pressure. Price competitively to start. Word of mouth spreads fast!
‚úì
Step 6: Deliver amazing results fast

‚úì
