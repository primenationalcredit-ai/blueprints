Step 1: Learn Domain Valuation Basics
Short domains (5-7 letters) are more valuable. .com is king. Avoid hyphens and numbers. Brandable or keyword-rich names sell best. Study sold domains on NameBio to learn patterns!
‚úì

‚úì

Check NameBio for comparable sales. Google the keywords‚Äîis there search demand? Who might want this domain? Don't register randomly‚Äîhave a buyer in mind!
‚úì

üí° Pro Tips & Warnings

üí° NameBio is Your Best Friend

Study past sales on NameBio.com before buying anything. See what similar domains sold for. This prevents overpaying and helps you spot opportunities others miss!

üí° Drop Losers After Year One

Don't renew domains nobody wants. If no offers or inquiries after 12 months, let it expire. Renewal fees add up. Focus budget on new acquisitions instead!
