üìã Step-by-Step Instructions

‚úì
Step 1: Pick your 48-hour window
Choose a weekend or two consecutive days where you have minimal obligations. Put it in your calendar!
‚úì
Step 2: Do a "pre-challenge" grocery run
Stock up on essentials BEFORE your 48 hours start. Meals, snacks, coffee - everything you need to avoid temptation.
‚úì
Step 3: Leave your wallet at home (or hide it)
Remove temptation completely. No cards, no cash in your pocket. If you MUST go out, bring only your ID.
‚úì
Step 4: Delete shopping apps (or log out)
Amazon, Target, food delivery apps - delete them or at least log out so you can't one-click purchase.
‚úì
Step 5: Plan FREE activities
Boredom leads to spending! Plan ahead: hikes, library visits, home workouts, calling friends, cooking new recipes.
‚úì
Step 6: Tell someone about your challenge
Accountability is powerful! Tell a friend, family member, or post on social media. They'll help keep you honest.
‚úì
Step 7: Start your 48 hours - YOU CAN DO THIS!
When you get the urge to buy something, write it down instead. If you still want it after 48 hours, reconsider then.
