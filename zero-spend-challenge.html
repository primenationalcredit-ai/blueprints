Why This Works

Most spending is automatic - we swipe without thinking. The Zero-Spend Challenge forces a 48-hour pause that breaks this autopilot. It proves you CAN survive without buying things, and shows you how much you spend unconsciously.

Before You Start (Do This NOW)

  • Check your fridge - make sure you have food for 2 days
  • Pack tomorrow's lunch tonight
  • Fill up your water bottle
  • Leave your credit cards at home
  • Delete shopping apps from your phone (temporarily)
  • Tell your accountability partner you're starting
  • Set a timer/reminder for 48 hours from now

During the Challenge

  • Morning Day 1: Make coffee at home, pack lunch
  • Afternoon Day 1: Resist the snack run - drink water instead
  • Evening Day 1: Cook dinner from what you have
  • Morning Day 2: Repeat - you're halfway there!
  • Afternoon Day 2: Stay strong - almost done!
  • Evening Day 2: Celebrate with a home-cooked meal!
üí° Survival Tips: When tempted, wait 10 minutes - the urge usually passes. Drink water when you feel like buying a snack. Call a friend instead of scrolling shopping apps. Remember WHY you're doing this!

When You're Tempted

  • Ask yourself: "Do I NEED this or WANT this?"
  • Text your accountability partner instead of buying
  • Add it to a list - if you still want it in 48 hours, reconsider
  • Leave the store/close the app immediately
  • Go for a walk to clear your head

After the Challenge

  • Calculate how much you saved
  • Transfer that amount to your savings account
  • Write down what you learned
  • Schedule your next challenge (try weekly!)
  • Share your success with your accountability partner
