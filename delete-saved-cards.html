Remove saved payment methods to stop impulse buying

Why This Works

When you have to manually enter your card number for every purchase, it creates friction that prevents impulse buying. Most people abandon their cart when they have to get up and find their wallet. That friction = money saved.

üí∞ Impact: Studies show removing saved cards reduces online impulse purchases by 60-80%!

Shopping Sites - Delete Cards Here

  • Amazon - Account > Payment Methods > Delete all cards
  • Target - Account > Wallet > Remove cards
  • Walmart - Account > Wallet > Delete
  • eBay - Account > Payments > Remove cards
  • Etsy - Account > Billing > Remove payment methods
  • Best Buy - Account > Payment Methods > Delete
  • Home Depot - My Account > Payment Methods > Remove

Food Delivery Apps - Delete Cards Here

  • DoorDash - Account > Payment > Remove all cards
  • Uber Eats - Wallet > Delete payment method
  • Grubhub - Account > Payment > Remove cards
  • Instacart - Account > Payment methods > Remove
  • Postmates - Payment > Delete cards

Digital Wallets - Delete Cards Here

  • Google Pay - pay.google.com > Payment methods > Remove
  • Apple Pay - Settings > Wallet & Apple Pay > Remove cards
  • PayPal - Wallet > Remove cards (keep bank for emergencies)
  • Venmo - Settings > Payment Methods > Remove cards
  • Cash App - Banking > Remove linked cards

Browser Autofill - Delete Cards Here

  • Chrome - Settings > Autofill > Payment methods > Delete all
  • Safari - Preferences > AutoFill > Credit Cards > Edit > Remove all
  • Firefox - Settings > Privacy & Security > Saved Credit Cards > Remove
  • Edge - Settings > Profiles > Payment info > Delete all
