Remove saved payment methods to stop impulse buying

Why This Works

When you have to manually enter your card number for every purchase, it creates friction that prevents impulse buying. Most people abandon their cart when they have to get up and find their wallet.

Where to Delete Saved Cards

  • Amazon - Account > Payment Methods > Delete all cards
  • Target - Account > Wallet > Remove cards
  • Walmart - Account > Wallet > Delete
  • DoorDash - Account > Payment > Remove
  • Uber Eats - Wallet > Delete payment method
  • Instacart - Account > Payment methods > Remove
  • Google Pay - pay.google.com > Payment methods
  • Apple Pay - Settings > Wallet & Apple Pay > Remove cards
  • PayPal - Wallet > Remove cards (keep bank for emergencies)
  • Browser autofill - Chrome/Safari settings > Payment methods > Delete all
üí° Pro Tip: After deleting, DO NOT re-save cards when prompted. Click "No thanks" every time!

Browser Autofill Instructions

Chrome

Settings > Autofill and passwords > Payment methods > Delete all saved cards

Safari

Preferences > AutoFill > Credit Cards > Edit > Remove all

Firefox

Settings > Privacy & Security > Saved Credit Cards > Remove All
