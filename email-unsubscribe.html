Why Marketing Emails Hurt

Every "SALE ENDS TODAY!" email is designed to create urgency and FOMO. They're not informing you - they're manipulating you into spending money you didn't plan to spend.

Goal: Unsubscribe from 10+ marketing emails today. Remove the daily temptation from your inbox.

Method 1: Manual Unsubscribe (15 min)

  1. Open your email inbox
  2. Search for "unsubscribe" - this finds marketing emails
  3. Open each email and scroll to the bottom
  4. Click the tiny "Unsubscribe" link
  5. Confirm on the website
  6. Repeat 10+ times

Method 2: Use Unroll.me (5 min)

  1. Go to Unroll.me
  2. Connect your email account
  3. See ALL your subscriptions in one list
  4. One-click unsubscribe from each
  5. Free and fast!
