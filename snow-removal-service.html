üíµ Pricing Strategies

How to Price Your Services

  • Per-push pricing: $25-50 base (up to 4"), +$15-20 per additional 4"
  • Seasonal contracts: Estimate 10-15 snowfalls √ó per-push rate, discount 10-15%
  • Size tiers: Small driveway $25-35, Standard $35-50, Large $50-75+
  • Emergency/priority: +50% for same-day or "before work" priority
  • Bundle discount: Driveway + walkway + salting = 10% off total

üí° Pro Tips

  • Dense routes: 10 clients on one street > 10 spread across town
  • Early bird wins: Clear before 7am for commuters. They'll pay premium.
  • Text alerts: "Heading to your place in 20 min" = professional service
  • Seasonal contracts: Sell Oct-Nov. Guaranteed money even in light winters.
  • Target seniors: Can't shovel themselves. Reliable recurring clients.
  • Stack services: Offer holiday lights, gutter cleaning for off-season income.

‚ö†Ô∏è Important Warnings

  • Liability: Someone slips on property you cleared = problem. Consider insurance.
  • Reliability is everything: Miss one storm and you lose that client forever.
  • Watch the forecast: Be ready to work at 4am when needed.
  • Physical demands: This is hard work. Stay hydrated, take breaks.
  • Salt storage: Keep salt dry. Wet salt clumps and won't spread.
