Step 1: Find ALL Your Subscriptions

  • Check your bank statement for recurring charges
  • Check your credit card statements
  • Check Apple App Store subscriptions (Settings > Your Name > Subscriptions)
  • Check Google Play subscriptions (Play Store > Menu > Subscriptions)
  • Check PayPal recurring payments
  • Check Amazon Subscribe & Save

Phone Cancellation Script

When They Answer:

"Hi, I'd like to cancel my subscription, please."

When They Ask Why:

"I'm cutting expenses and I'm not using it enough to justify the cost."

When They Offer a Discount:

"I appreciate that, but I'd still like to cancel. I can always resubscribe later if I need it."

If They Push Back:

"I understand you're trying to help, but my decision is final. Please process the cancellation now."

üí° Retention Offer Tips: Companies often offer 50-75% off to keep you. Only accept if you ACTUALLY use the service weekly. Set a reminder to cancel before the discount ends. Saying "let me think about it" often gets you an even better offer!

Step 2: Cancel These Subscriptions

  • Streaming services you haven't watched this week
  • Gym membership if you haven't gone 4+ times this month
  • Subscription boxes (they're almost never worth it)
  • Premium app versions when free works fine
  • Magazine/news subscriptions you don't read
  • Cloud storage beyond what you actually need
  • Meal kit services (way more expensive than groceries)
  • Any "free trial" you forgot to cancel

Step 3: Move the Savings

  • Calculate total monthly savings from cancelled subscriptions
  • Set up automatic transfer of that amount to savings
  • Set calendar reminder to review subscriptions monthly
