üìã Step-by-Step Instructions

‚úì
Step 1: Check your bank/credit card statements
Look at the last 2-3 months. Search for recurring charges. Write down every subscription you find with its cost.
‚úì
Step 2: Check your email for receipts
Search your email for "subscription", "receipt", "invoice", "renewal". You'll find ones you forgot!
‚úì
Step 3: Check app store subscriptions
iPhone: Settings ‚Üí Your Name ‚Üí Subscriptions. Android: Play Store ‚Üí Menu ‚Üí Subscriptions.
‚úì
Step 4: Use the tracker below to categorize
Go through each subscription. Ask: "Have I used this in the last 30 days?" If no, it's a cancel candidate!
‚úì
Step 5: Cancel the unnecessary ones
For each one you're canceling, go to the account settings and cancel. Some may require a phone call.
‚úì
Step 6: Set a calendar reminder to audit again
Set a reminder for 3 months from now. Subscriptions creep back! Regular audits keep you lean.
