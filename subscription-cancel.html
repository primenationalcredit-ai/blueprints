üìû The Universal Cancellation Script

This script works for almost ANY subscription. Stay calm, polite, but FIRM!

üìû When You Call to Cancel:
YOU SAY:
"Hi, I'd like to cancel my subscription, please."
Be direct. Don't explain too much yet.
THEY'LL ASK:
"I'm sorry to hear that. Can I ask why you're canceling?"
YOU SAY:
"I'm cutting expenses and I'm not using it enough to justify the cost."
Keep it simple. Don't give them ammunition to argue.
THEY'LL OFFER:
"What if we offer you 50% off for the next 3 months?"
YOU SAY:
"I appreciate that, but I'd still like to cancel. I can always resubscribe later if I need it."
This phrase shuts down urgency tactics!
THEY'LL TRY:
"Are you sure? We have a pause option instead..."
YOU SAY:
"I'm sure. Please proceed with the cancellation and email me confirmation."
ALWAYS get confirmation in writing!
üí™ Power Phrases That Work Every Time:
"I'd like to cancel"
Direct and clear. No room for misunderstanding.
"I can always resubscribe later"
Shuts down urgency tactics instantly!
"Please email me confirmation"
Creates a paper trail. Protects you!
"I've made my decision"
Ends the negotiation. Shows you're firm.

üìã Your Step-by-Step Action Plan

‚úì
Step 1: Find ALL Your Subscriptions
You probably have more than you think! Let's find every single one.
üîç How to Find Every Subscription:
  • Check your bank/credit card statements - Go back 3 months. Look for recurring charges on the same day each month.
  • Check your email - Search for "subscription," "receipt," "billing," "renewal." You'll find forgotten ones!
  • Check your phone - iPhone: Settings ‚Üí [Your Name] ‚Üí Subscriptions. Android: Play Store ‚Üí Subscriptions.
  • Use a tool: Apps like Rocket Money or Trim automatically find subscriptions for you!
  • Common forgotten ones: Cloud storage, old streaming services, news sites, apps you downloaded once, gym memberships you don't use.
‚úì
Step 2: Decide: Cancel or Negotiate Each One
For each subscription, ask yourself two simple questions.
ü§î Ask Yourself:
  • Question 1: "Did I use this in the last 30 days?"
  • Question 2: "Is it worth the cost?"
  • If NO to either: CANCEL IT!
  • If YES to both but expensive: Try to NEGOTIATE a lower price!
  • Be honest: "I'll start using it" is usually a lie we tell ourselves. If you haven't used it, cancel it!
‚úì
Step 3: Try Online Cancellation First
Many subscriptions can be cancelled online in 2 minutes. Try this before calling!
üíª How to Cancel Online:
  • Log in to the service's website or app
  • Go to: Account ‚Üí Settings ‚Üí Subscription ‚Üí Billing ‚Üí Membership
  • Look for: "Cancel," "End subscription," "Manage membership"
  • iPhone subscriptions: Settings ‚Üí [Your Name] ‚Üí Subscriptions ‚Üí Tap the sub ‚Üí Cancel
  • Android subscriptions: Play Store ‚Üí Profile ‚Üí Payments ‚Üí Subscriptions ‚Üí Cancel
  • Use JustDelete.me - This website has direct links to cancel accounts on hundreds of services!
‚úì
Step 4: Call With Your Script Ready
If you have to call, have this playbook OPEN in front of you!
üìû Before You Call:
  • Have this playbook open on your phone or computer so you can read the scripts
  • Have your account info ready - account number, email, phone, last 4 of payment card
  • Be prepared to wait - some companies put you on hold hoping you'll hang up. DON'T!
  • Stay calm and polite - but FIRM. The person is doing their job. Be nice but don't give in.
  • Remember: "I appreciate that, but I've made my decision" works for almost everything!
‚úì
Step 5: GET CONFIRMATION IN WRITING!
This is CRITICAL! Without written proof, they might keep charging you.
üìß Always Get Proof:
  • On the phone: "Please email me confirmation of this cancellation."
  • Get a confirmation number and write it down!
  • Ask: "What is my final billing date? When does service end?"
  • Screenshot any online cancellation confirmations
  • Save the email in a folder called "Cancellations"
  • Why this matters: If they charge you again, you have proof to dispute it!
‚úì
Step 6: Check Next Month's Statement
Companies sometimes "accidentally" keep charging. Verify it actually stopped!
‚úÖ Verify Cancellation:
  • Set a calendar reminder for your next billing date to check
  • If you're still charged: Call back with your confirmation number
  • Dispute the charge with your bank if the company won't refund
  • This is why written confirmation matters!
‚úì
Step 7: Move Saved Money to Savings!
Make your cancellations PAY OFF visibly. Transfer the savings!
üí∞ Celebrate Your Savings:
  • Add up everything you cancelled - how much per month?
  • Set up an automatic transfer for that amount to your savings account
  • Example: Cancelled $75/month in subscriptions? Transfer $75 to savings on the 1st of each month!
  • Watch it grow: $75/month = $900/year in savings!
  • Celebrate: You did it! That money is now YOURS!
