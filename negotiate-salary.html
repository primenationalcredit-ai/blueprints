üí° Pro Tips

Maximize your success:

  • Never give the first number in a new job - let them anchor first
  • Use silence - After stating your number, wait. Don't fill the silence.
  • Negotiate more than salary - PTO, remote work, signing bonus, title
  • Get it in writing - Always confirm in email
  • Time it right - After big wins, during reviews, or when taking on more responsibility
  • Be willing to walk away - Know your bottom line
