üè† Room-by-Room Energy Savings

üç≥

Kitchen

Set fridge to 38-42¬∞F, freezer to 0-5¬∞F

Colder isn't better - it just wastes energy. Check with a thermometer.

Saves: $20-30/year

Keep fridge coils clean

Dusty coils make the compressor work harder. Clean every 6 months.

Saves: $15-25/year

Air dry dishes instead of heat dry

Open the dishwasher after the rinse cycle and let dishes air dry.

Saves: $10-20/year

Use small appliances when possible

Toaster ovens, slow cookers, and microwaves use less energy than the oven.

Saves: $20-40/year
üõÅ

Bathroom

Take shorter showers

Cutting just 2 minutes saves 5 gallons of hot water per shower.

Saves: $50-100/year

Fix running toilets

A running toilet can waste 200+ gallons per day! Usually just needs a $10 flapper.

Saves: $70-150/year

Turn off water while brushing teeth

Running water while brushing wastes 4+ gallons per session.

Saves: $20-30/year
üëï

Laundry Room

Wash in cold water

90% of washer energy goes to heating water. Modern detergents work great in cold!

Saves: $60-100/year

Clean the dryer lint trap every load

A clogged lint trap makes the dryer work 30% harder - plus it's a fire hazard!

Saves: $25-40/year

Use a clothesline when possible

Free energy from the sun! Your clothes will last longer too.

Saves: $50-100/year
üì∫

Living Room & Bedrooms

Use smart power strips for entertainment centers

TVs, gaming consoles, and cable boxes use power even when "off."

Saves: $50-75/year

Use ceiling fans strategically

Counterclockwise in summer (pushes air down), clockwise in winter (circulates warm air).

Saves: $40-60/year

Use blackout curtains

Keeps heat out in summer, cold out in winter. Reduces HVAC load significantly.

Saves: $30-50/year

‚òÄÔ∏è Summer Cooling Tips

  • Set thermostat to 78¬∞F when home
  • Use ceiling fans to feel 4¬∞F cooler
  • Close blinds on sunny windows
  • Avoid using oven - use microwave or grill
  • Run heat-producing appliances at night

‚ùÑÔ∏è Winter Heating Tips

  • Set thermostat to 68¬∞F when home
  • Lower to 60-65¬∞F when sleeping
  • Open blinds on sunny days for free heat
  • Close fireplace damper when not in use
  • Reverse ceiling fan direction (clockwise)
  • Seal drafts around doors and windows
  • Use space heaters for occupied rooms only
