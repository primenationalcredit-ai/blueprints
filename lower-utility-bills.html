  • Step 1: Get a home energy audit (often FREE!)
    Many utility companies offer free energy audits. They'll tell you exactly where you're wasting energy!
    ‚è±Ô∏è Call your utility Often free
  • Step 2: Check for air leaks around doors/windows
    Seal gaps with weatherstripping or caulk. Cold/hot air leaking in wastes money!
    How to find leaks:
    • Feel for drafts around windows and doors
    • Hold a candle near edges - flickering = leak
    • Check outlets on exterior walls
    • Look for daylight around door frames
