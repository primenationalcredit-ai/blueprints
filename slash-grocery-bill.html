üí° The Golden Rules of Grocery Savings

üìù Rule #1: Always Shop with a List

Impulse buys add 20-30% to your bill. Write a list and STICK TO IT. No exceptions!

Saves: $50-100/month

üçΩÔ∏è Rule #2: Meal Plan Before Shopping

Plan 5-7 dinners before you shop. You'll buy only what you need and waste less food.

Saves: $75-150/month

üö´ Rule #3: Never Shop Hungry

Hungry shoppers spend 64% more on average. Eat before you go!

Saves: $30-50/month

üè∑Ô∏è Rule #4: Check Unit Prices

Bigger isn't always cheaper. Compare price per ounce, not package price.

Saves: $20-40/month

ü•´ Rule #5: Buy Store Brands

Store brands are 25-40% cheaper and often made by the same manufacturers!

Saves: $40-80/month

‚ùÑÔ∏è Rule #6: Embrace Frozen & Canned

Frozen vegetables are just as nutritious, cheaper, and don't go bad!

Saves: $30-60/month

Download cashback apps (Ibotta, Fetch, Checkout 51)

‚úì
Find your cheapest local stores

‚úì
Plan meals around sales and what's in season
Check weekly ads before meal planning. Build meals around what's on sale!
‚úì
Batch cook and meal prep on weekends
Cooking in bulk saves time AND money. Make enough for leftovers!
‚úì
Use the freezer strategically
Buy meat when it's on sale and freeze it. Freeze bread, bananas, and leftovers.
‚úì
Have 2-3 "emergency" cheap meals memorized
Pasta, rice & beans, eggs - know quick cheap meals to avoid takeout temptation.
