Coupon stacking means combining multiple discounts on the SAME purchase - store coupons + manufacturer coupons + cash back apps + credit card rewards. Expert stackers routinely save 40-70% on groceries and household items. This isn't extreme couponing - it's smart shopping!

üí° The Golden Rule

You can almost ALWAYS use ONE store coupon + ONE manufacturer coupon on the SAME item. This is the foundation of stacking. Then add cash back apps on top!

üìã Your Stacking Action Plan

‚úì
Step 1: Download store loyalty apps
Get Target Circle, Kroger app, CVS ExtraCare, Walgreens, etc. These have digital store coupons that stack with everything else. Clip coupons BEFORE you shop!
‚úì
Step 2: Download cash back apps
Get Ibotta, Fetch Rewards, Checkout 51, and Shopkick. You can scan the SAME receipt in multiple apps for different offers!
‚úì
Step 3: Check weekly ads for sales
Sales run Sunday-Saturday. Check Flipp app or store websites. Never pay full price - items cycle on sale every 6-8 weeks.
‚úì
Step 4: Find manufacturer coupons
Check coupons.com, newspaper inserts, brand websites, and "text to save" offers on packaging. These stack with store coupons!
‚úì
Step 5: Build your stack before shopping
For each item: Sale price + store coupon + manufacturer coupon + cash back offer. Plan your trip around the best stacks!
‚úì
Step 6: Use the right credit card
Use a card with grocery bonus category (5-6% at grocery stores) or at minimum 2% cash back. This stacks on top of everything!
‚úì
Step 7: Scan receipts immediately
After shopping, scan your receipt in Ibotta, Fetch, Checkout 51, etc. Most offers expire - don't wait! Do it in the parking lot.
‚úì
Step 8: Track your savings
Keep a running total of what you saved. This keeps you motivated and helps you identify your best stacking opportunities.
