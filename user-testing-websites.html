‚úì
Step 1: Check your equipment
You need: computer with webcam, microphone (built-in is fine), quiet space, reliable internet. Most platforms require Chrome or their app.
‚úì
Step 2: Sign up for multiple platforms
Join UserTesting, Userlytics, TryMyUI, and dscout. More platforms = more test opportunities. Each has different clients.
‚úì
Step 3: Complete qualification tests
Most platforms require a sample test. Take your time! Speak clearly, think out loud, give genuine feedback. First impression matters.

Step 4: Fill out all profile/screener info
Complete demographics thoroughly. Tests target specific users - the more info you provide, the more tests you'll qualify for.
‚úì
Step 5: Check for tests frequently
Tests fill up FAST - often within minutes! Check multiple times daily. Turn on notifications. Morning and evening are best times.
‚úì
Step 6: Master the "think aloud" technique
Narrate EVERYTHING: "I'm looking for the cart... I see this button... I'm confused about..." Don't just click silently. More talking = higher ratings!
‚úì
Step 7: Give honest, constructive feedback
Companies want real opinions, not praise. Be honest about confusion, frustration, likes and dislikes. Specific feedback gets you invited to more tests.
‚úì
Step 8: Build your rating and get more tests
High-quality tests = higher ratings = more test invitations. Treat each test professionally. Consistent quality leads to consistent income!
