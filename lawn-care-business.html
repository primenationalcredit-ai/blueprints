üìã Your Lawn Care Action Plan

‚úì
Step 1: Get basic equipment
Start with a reliable mower, string trimmer, and blower. Buy used to save money! $400-800 total gets you started.
‚úì
Step 2: Get liability insurance
General liability insurance is essential ($300-500/year). Protects you if something goes wrong. Many customers require it.
‚úì
Step 3: Set up business basics
Business license ($50-200), separate bank account, simple invoicing (Wave is free), Google Business Profile (essential and free!).
‚úì
Step 4: Set your prices
Research local competition. Price by lawn size. Aim for $1/minute minimum ($60/hour). First job? Quote slightly lower to get started.
‚úì
Step 5: Get your first customers
Start with neighbors, post on Nextdoor, Facebook groups. Door hangers in nice neighborhoods work great! Tell everyone you know.
‚úì
Step 6: Create efficient routes
Group customers by neighborhood. Less driving = more mowing = more money! Try to get 3-5 lawns in each area.
‚úì
Step 7: Deliver excellent service
Clean cuts, consistent schedule, blow off all surfaces. Mow in different patterns each week. Leave the property looking PERFECT.
‚úì
Step 8: Scale up
Add services (mulching, hedges, cleanups), upgrade to commercial equipment, eventually hire help. Target commercial accounts (offices, HOAs).
