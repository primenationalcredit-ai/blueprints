üîÑ Your Options

Choose based on your credit score and situation:

0% Balance Transfer Card
0% APR for 12-21 months

Transfer credit card debt to a new card with 0% intro APR. Pay it off before the promo ends!

Personal Loan
7-20% APR (fixed)

Consolidate all debts into one fixed monthly payment. Know exactly when you'll be debt-free.

Credit Union Loan
Often lowest rates
Credit unions are non-profit and often offer better rates than banks, especially if your credit isn't perfect.

Negotiate with Current Cards
Free to try!
Call your credit card company and ask for a lower rate. Works surprisingly often!

üìã Step-by-Step: Lower Your Rates

  • Step 1: List all your debts with interest rates
    Know what you're working with. List each debt, balance, interest rate, and minimum payment.
    ‚è±Ô∏è 15 minutes Important!
  • Step 2: Check your credit score
    Your score determines which options are available. Check free at Credit Karma or your bank.
    Score guides options:
    • 720+: Best balance transfer cards, lowest personal loan rates
    • 670-719: Good options still available
    • 580-669: Credit union or secured loans may be best
    • Below 580: Focus on building credit first, or try negotiating
    ‚è±Ô∏è 5 minutes Free to check
  • Step 3: Call current credit cards and ask for lower rate
    This is FREE and works more often than you'd think! Just call and ask.
    What to say:
    • "Hi, I've been a customer for [X years] and I'd like to request a lower interest rate on my card."
    • "I've received offers for cards with lower rates and I'm considering transferring my balance."
    • "Is there anything you can do to lower my current rate?"
    • If they say no: "Can I speak with a supervisor?" or "Is there a hardship program?"
    ‚è±Ô∏è 15 min per card Often works!
  • Step 4: Compare balance transfer offers
    Look for 0% APR intro offers. Note the transfer fee (usually 3-5%) and how long the 0% lasts.
    ‚è±Ô∏è 20 minutes NerdWallet has good comparisons
  • Step 5: Compare personal loan offers
    Check rates at multiple lenders. Many let you prequalify without affecting your credit!
    ‚è±Ô∏è 20 minutes Soft pull = no credit impact
  • Step 6: Choose best option and apply
    Pick the option that saves you the most. Apply and use the funds to pay off high-interest debt!
