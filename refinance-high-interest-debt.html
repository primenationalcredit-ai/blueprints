üìù Emergency Refinancing Steps

‚úì

Step 1: Stop the Bleeding

STOP using high-interest cards immediately. Cut them up or freeze them. You can't fill a bucket with a hole in it.

‚úì

Step 2: List All High-Interest Debts

Everything over 15%: credit cards, store cards, personal loans, payday loans. Get exact balances and rates.

‚úì

Step 3: Check Your Credit Score

Free at Credit Karma, Experian, or your bank. This determines your options. Even fair credit (580+) has options.

‚úì

Step 4: Apply for 0% Balance Transfer (if good credit)

Best option if you can pay off in 12-21 months. Cards like Chase Slate, Citi Simplicity offer 0% intro APR.

‚úì

Step 5: Get Personal Loan Quotes

Compare 5+ lenders. Many do soft-pull prequalification. SoFi, LightStream, Upgrade, Prosper, credit unions.

‚úì

Step 6: Accept Best Offer & Pay Off Cards

Transfer or pay off high-interest debt immediately. Don't let the money sit‚Äîpay it off same day.

‚úì

Step 7: Automate New Payment

Set up autopay for new loan. Pay more than minimum to eliminate faster. Never miss a payment.

üí° Maximum Impact Strategies

  • Attack highest rate first: If you can't refinance everything, prioritize 24%+ debt
  • Use bonus/tax refund: Put windfalls toward principal after refinancing
  • Negotiate existing cards: Call and ask for rate reduction‚Äîsometimes works
  • 0% card + side hustle: Combine 0% with extra income to crush debt fast
  • Credit union membership: Join for best rates (easy to qualify)

‚ö†Ô∏è Critical Warnings

  • Don't run up cards again: This is the #1 failure. Close or freeze paid-off cards.
