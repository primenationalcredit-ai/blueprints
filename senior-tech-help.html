‚ù§Ô∏è Tips for Working with Seniors

A little patience goes a LONG way!

Do's:

  • Be patient - Explain things slowly and repeat if needed
  • Write things down - Give them notes they can reference later
  • Use big font - Increase text size on their devices
  • Show, then let them try - Hands-on practice helps them remember
  • Make it about connection - "Now you can video call your grandkids!"
  • Be genuinely kind - They can tell if you're rushing

Don'ts:

  • Don't use jargon - "tap here" not "access the settings menu"
  • Don't make them feel dumb - They're not, tech is just new to them
  • Don't rush - Build in extra time for your appointments
  • Don't just fix it silently - Explain what you're doing so they learn
