üîß Equipment Needed

Basic Starter Kit ($100-300)

  • Extension ladder: 24-28 ft for 2-story homes ($150-300, or borrow/rent)
  • Gutter scoop: Plastic scoop for debris ($5-10)
  • Bucket or bag: Attach to ladder for debris collection ($10-20)
  • Work gloves: Heavy duty ($10-15)
  • Safety glasses: Protect from debris ($5-10)
  • Garden hose + nozzle: For flushing after cleaning ($20-30)

Advanced (Optional)

  • Gutter vacuum system: Clean from ground level ($200-500)
  • Pressure washer: For add-on services
  • Leaf blower: For dry debris, roof clearing

üí° Pro Tips

  • Target tree-heavy streets: More trees = more gutter clogs = more customers
  • Book next appointment: "Want me to come back in spring?" before you leave
  • Leave door hangers: After a job, hit neighbors on both sides
  • Bundle services: "Gutter cleaning + window wash = 10% off"
  • Annual reminder: Email past customers before each season

‚ö†Ô∏è Safety First

  • Ladder safety: 4:1 rule (1 ft out for every 4 ft up). Always secure.
  • Check roof edges: Soft or damaged areas can collapse
  • Power lines: NEVER put ladder near electrical wires
  • Wet conditions: Don't work on wet, icy, or windy days
  • Insurance: General liability ($300-500/yr). Smart investment.
