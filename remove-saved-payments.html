üéØ Why Remove Saved Payments?

"One-click buying" is designed to make spending so easy you don't think. When you have to manually enter your card number, you have time to ask: "Do I really need this?"

Studies show that adding just 20 seconds of friction to a purchase reduces impulse buying by up to 50%. Typing in a 16-digit card number = lots of friction!

üõí Walmart Instructions:
