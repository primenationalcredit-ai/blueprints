üõí Major Shopping Sites

üü† Amazon

The biggest one-click offender. Here's how to remove:

  • Go to Amazon.com ‚Üí Account ‚Üí Payment Methods
  • Click "Edit" next to each card ‚Üí "Remove"
  • Also disable: 1-Click ordering in Account Settings
  • Remove Amazon Pay from other sites too

üéØ Target

  • Target.com ‚Üí Account ‚Üí Wallet
  • Remove all saved cards
  • Also check Target Circle for linked cards

üîµ Walmart

  • Walmart.com ‚Üí Account ‚Üí Wallet
  • Delete all payment methods
  • Also remove from Walmart+ if subscribed

üõçÔ∏è Other Major Sites

  • eBay: Account ‚Üí Payments ‚Üí Remove cards
  • Etsy: Account Settings ‚Üí Billing ‚Üí Payment Methods
  • Best Buy: Account ‚Üí Payment Methods
  • Macy's, Nordstrom, etc.: Account ‚Üí Wallet/Payment

üì± Digital Wallets & Browsers

üí≥ PayPal

  • Log into PayPal ‚Üí Wallet ‚Üí Click each card ‚Üí Remove
  • Turn off "One Touch" in Settings
  • Consider keeping only debit card (not credit)

üçé Apple Pay

  • iPhone: Settings ‚Üí Wallet & Apple Pay ‚Üí Remove cards
  • Mac: System Settings ‚Üí Wallet & Apple Pay
  • Safari Autofill: Settings ‚Üí Safari ‚Üí AutoFill ‚Üí Saved Credit Cards

ü§ñ Google Pay / Chrome

  • pay.google.com ‚Üí Payment Methods ‚Üí Remove cards
  • Chrome: Settings ‚Üí Payment methods ‚Üí Turn off "Save and fill payment methods"
  • Delete any saved cards from Chrome

ü¶ä Firefox / Other Browsers

  • Settings ‚Üí Privacy & Security ‚Üí Forms and Autofill
  • Turn off "Save credit cards"
  • Delete any existing saved cards

üí° Pro Tips

üéØ Maximize Friction

  • Use a virtual card for subscriptions only - makes tracking easier
  • Keep your physical card in a drawer, not your wallet
  • Memorize nothing - the inconvenience is the point
  • Use Privacy.com to create single-use card numbers
  • Set a rule: If you have to enter the card number, wait 24 hours

‚ö†Ô∏è Before You Delete

  • Check for active subscriptions - update billing info first or cancel
  • Note autopay bills - keep those payment methods or update them
  • Keep one emergency method if you need to (debit card with low limit)
  • Screenshot saved cards if you need the card numbers later
