What is a 401(k) Match?

Many employers will MATCH what you contribute to your retirement account, up to a certain percentage. This is literally FREE MONEY added to your retirement savings.

üí∞ Example - FREE MONEY:

You earn $50,000/year
Employer matches 50% up to 6% of your salary
You contribute 6% = $3,000/year
Employer adds 50% match = $1,500 FREE

That's $1,500 in free money every year!

Action Steps

Step 1: Find Out Your Match

Ask HR or check your benefits portal: "What is the 401(k) match?" Common matches: 50% up to 6%, 100% up to 3%, dollar-for-dollar up to 4%

Step 2: Check Your Current Contribution

Log into your 401(k) portal or check your pay stub to see what percentage you're currently contributing

Step 3: Increase to Get Full Match

If you're not contributing enough to get the FULL match, increase your contribution TODAY
