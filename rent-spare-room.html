üì± Where to List

Listing Platforms

  • Airbnb: Short-term rentals. 3% host fee. Largest audience for travelers.
  • VRBO: Similar to Airbnb. Often attracts families. 3-5% fee.
  • Furnished Finder: Medium-term rentals for traveling professionals. $99/year.
  • Facebook Marketplace: Long-term roommates. Free. Large local audience.
  • Craigslist: Long-term roommates. Free. Screen carefully.
  • Roomies/SpareRoom: Roommate matching platforms. Free or low cost.

üí° Pro Hosting Tips

  • Stock essentials: Toiletries, coffee, snacks = 5-star reviews
  • Quick communication: Fast responses lead to more bookings
  • House rules: Clear expectations prevent problems
  • Smart lock: Keyless entry = easier for everyone
  • Local guidebook: Restaurant/activity recommendations impress guests

‚ö†Ô∏è Important Considerations

  • Insurance: Standard homeowner's may not cover guests. Check your policy.
  • Taxes: Rental income is taxable. Track expenses for deductions.
  • HOA/Lease: Many prohibit short-term rentals. Check first!
  • Screen carefully: Bad roommate/guest can be a nightmare
  • Privacy: You're sharing YOUR home. Set boundaries.
