üìã Step-by-Step: Getting Started

Follow these steps to get your first pet clients!

  • Step 1: Walk dogs for friends/neighbors for free
    Get some experience and references. Ask them for a review when done!
    ‚è±Ô∏è 1-2 weeks Easy
  • Step 2: Create profiles on pet sitting apps
    Rover and Wag! are the biggest. They bring clients to you and handle payments.

    Top pet sitting apps:

    • Rover - Biggest platform, most clients. They take 20% fee.
    • Wag! - Good for dog walking specifically. They take 40% fee.
    • Care.com - Also lists pet care. Less competition.
    • Go direct eventually - After you build clients, work directly with them!
    ‚è±Ô∏è 30-60 minutes Easy
  • Step 3: Write a great profile with photos
    Include photos of you with dogs! Mention any experience, and be warm and friendly.

    Profile must-haves:

    • Smiling photo of you with a dog
    • Mention if you have pets of your own
    • Why you love animals
    • Your availability
    • Any experience (even if just with family pets!)
    ‚è±Ô∏è 30 minutes Important!
  • Step 4: Set competitive prices
    Start slightly below average to get your first reviews. Raise prices once you have 5+ great reviews.
    ‚è±Ô∏è 15 minutes Easy
  • Step 5: Do a meet & greet with every new pet
    Before the first service, meet the pet and owner. This builds trust and helps you understand the pet's needs.
    ‚è±Ô∏è 15-30 min each Critical!
  • Step 6: Send photo updates during service
    Pet owners LOVE getting photos of their babies! Send a cute photo after every walk or visit.
