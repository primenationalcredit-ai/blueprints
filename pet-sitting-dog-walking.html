üéÑ Holiday Strategy

Holiday Periods = 2-3x Normal Income

  • Book 2-3 months early: Regular clients get first dibs, then open to new clients
  • Raise rates 50-100%: Everyone does, demand is sky-high
  • Stack boarders: If you can handle 3 dogs, that's $120-250+/night
  • Multi-day minimum: Require 3+ night minimum during peak holidays
  • Key holidays: Thanksgiving, Christmas, New Year's, July 4th, Spring Break
  • Deposits: Collect 50% deposit for holiday bookings (prevents cancellations)

üí° Pro Sitter Tips

  • Daily photo/video updates: Owners LOVE seeing their pets happy. Builds loyalty.
  • Learn pet personalities: Remember quirks, favorite toys, routines. Personal touch wins.
  • Emergency prep: Know nearest 24/7 vet, have owner's vet info, know pet allergies
  • Accept extended bookings: 2-week vacation? That's $700-1,400 for one client!
  • Build direct clients: After platform trust, offer 10% off for direct booking

‚ö†Ô∏è Critical Warnings

  • Never skip meet & greets: One bad fit can mean injury, escapes, or terrible reviews
  • Insurance is non-negotiable: One incident without it could cost you thousands
  • Know your limits: 3 dogs is usually max. Overwhelmed = bad care = bad reviews
  • Separate dogs unless approved: Dogs meeting for first time need supervision
  • Document everything: Photos at drop-off, notes on health, any concerns in writing
