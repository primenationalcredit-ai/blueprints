üìã Your Wedding Officiant Action Plan

‚úì
Step 1: Get ordained online
Universal Life Church or American Marriage Ministries - free and instant! Takes 5 minutes. You're legally able to perform ceremonies in most states.
‚úì
Step 2: Research your state's requirements
Google "[Your State] wedding officiant requirements." Some states need county registration, Letter of Good Standing, or credentials filed. Know before you perform!
‚úì
Step 3: Develop your ceremony offerings
Create 2-3 service packages. Simple ceremony, custom ceremony, full planning. Write sample scripts. Religious, secular, interfaith - define your style!
‚úì
Step 4: Set your pricing
Research local rates. Start slightly below average to build reviews. Include what's in each package. Add rehearsal, travel, rush fees.
‚úì
Step 5: Create your online presence
List on The Knot, WeddingWire (free listings). Create Google Business Profile. Set up Instagram. Simple website optional but helpful.
‚úì
Step 6: Book your first weddings
Friends, family, referrals count! Offer lower rates to start building portfolio. Each ceremony = more experience + reviews.
‚úì
Step 7: Deliver amazing ceremonies
Meet couples beforehand. Practice the ceremony out loud. Arrive 30+ minutes early. Bring backup script. Know names of wedding party. Stay calm - you set the tone!
‚úì
Step 8: Collect reviews and raise prices
Ask every couple for reviews on The Knot/WeddingWire/Google. As reviews build, raise your rates. Established officiants charge $500-1,000+ per ceremony!
